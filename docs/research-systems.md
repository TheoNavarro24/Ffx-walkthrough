# FFX Game Systems Research

## Sphere Grid (Standard Grid)

### Per-Character Natural Paths
- **Tidus**: Speed/Strength focus. Learns Haste, Slow, Delay Buster, Cheer, Flee. Grid runs into Yuna's.
- **Yuna**: Magic/Evasion focus. White Magic (Cure line, Regen, Esuna, Reflect, Shell, Life, Full Life, Holy). Grid runs into Rikku's.
- **Wakka**: Accuracy/Strength. Status Attacks (Sleep, Silence, Dark, etc.). Grid runs into Lulu's late.
- **Lulu**: Black Magic (Fire/Ice/Thunder/Water lines up to -aga, then Ultima, Bio, Drain, etc.). Magic stat focus.
- **Kimahri**: Flexible! Connects to every grid except Auron's. Jack of all trades.
- **Auron**: Strength/HP. Physical powerhouse with Break abilities (Armor, Mental, Power, Magic).
- **Rikku**: Steal, Use, Mix. Agility/MP focus. Grid runs into Tidus's.

### Kimahri Strategy (IMPORTANT)
Best approach: Send to Rikku's grid early via Lv.1 Key Spheres:
1. First: Get Steal and Use from Rikku's area
2. Then: Move toward Yuna's for Reflect/Dispel
3. Then: Head to beginning of Tidus's grid for Flee and Haste
4. Finally: Veer to Auron's grid (backwards) for Break abilities

### Key Milestones
- By Mi'ihen: Tidus should have Haste soon
- By Macalania: Yuna should have Curaga
- Mt. Gagazet: Steal 4-5 Lv.3 Key Spheres from Biran/Yenke
- Post-airship: Use Lv.4 Key Spheres for cross-grid unlocking (Quick Hit for Tidus)

## Monster Arena

### Structure
- 13 area sections across Spira
- Capture by killing with Capture weapons (buy from Arena owner in Calm Lands)
- Need to capture 1 of each fiend type per area

### Unlock Categories
1. **Area Conquest**: Capture all fiends in a specific area
2. **Species Conquest**: Capture all fiends of a specific type across all areas
3. **Original**: Special creations from other combinations

### Key Rewards
- Mars Sigil (Auron): Unlock 10 Area/Species Conquest monsters
- Nirvana (Yuna): Capture all Calm Lands fiends (after Celestial Mirror)
- Blossom Crown: Capture all Mt. Gagazet fiends

### Endgame Farming (from handoff, likely correct)
- Don Tonberry: AP farming (Overdrive->AP trick)
- Ironclad: HP Sphere farming
- Juggernaut: Strength Sphere farming

## Optional Aeons

### Yojimbo
- Location: Cavern of the Stolen Fayth (N of Calm Lands)
- Method: Negotiate. Choose "defeating powerful enemies"
- Target: ~190,000 Gil
- Usage: Pay Gil per attack (more = stronger, Zanmato is instant kill)

### Anima
- Location: Baaj Temple (airship coordinates/search)
- Requires: All 6 Destruction Sphere items from all temples
- Guard: Geosgaeno boss fight
- Most powerful damage Aeon

### Magus Sisters
- Location: Remiem Temple
- Requires:
  1. Flower Scepter (beat all Belgemine Aeons)
  2. Blossom Crown (capture all Mt. Gagazet fiends for Monster Arena)
- Most powerful summon overall (Cindy's Passado)
