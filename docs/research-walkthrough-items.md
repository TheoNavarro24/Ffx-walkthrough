# FFX Walkthrough Items Research — Per Location

## Sources: WebSearch snippets from Jegged, Gamer Guides, Game8, StrategyWiki, EIP Gaming, GameFAQs snippets

## 1. Zanarkand Prologue
- No collectible items during the prologue blitzball/Sinspawn Ammes section
- Boss: Sinspawn Ammes (tutorial, Auron joins)

## 2. Submerged Ruins + Salvage Ship

### Submerged Ruins
- 2 Potions (treasure chest south of Al Bhed Compilation Sphere)
- 200 Gil (large dry area, eastern edge)
- Hi-Potion (western catwalk)
- X-Potion (south along wall)
- Ether (hidden chest in narrow stairwell, nearest camera)
- Withered Bouquet key item (examine vase at top of stairs)
- Hi-Potion (chest on balcony)
- Boss: Klikk (HP: 1,500)
- Boss: Tros (HP: 2,200)

### Salvage Ship
- 200 Gil (right side of first area)
- Potion x2 (upper left floor)
- Item in upper right corner of deck
- 3 Potions (talk to Al Bhed on left)
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. I** (back of ship on ground)

## 3. Besaid Island

### Beach
- **Moon Crest** (east alcove, swim to chest — needed for Yuna's Celestial Weapon)
- Antidote (left side of beach)
- 200 Gil, 5 Potions, Hi-Potion (talk to NPCs)

### Underwater/River
- Phoenix Down (swim south, dive near western wall)
- Hi-Potion (further east in water)
- 2 Antidotes (along waterway)

### Besaid Village
- Phoenix Down (immediate chest)
- 2 Potions, Hi-Potion, 400 Gil (hidden path behind first hut on left)
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. II** (Crusaders' Lodge, floor)

### Besaid Temple Cloister
- **Rod of Wisdom** (Destruction Sphere treasure — MISSABLE, Dark Valefor blocks return in HD)

### Bosses: None here (Valefor obtained as first Aeon)

## 4. S.S. Liki
- Remedy (room across from O'aka)
- Up to 20 Potions (kick suitcase on ground repeatedly)
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. III** (Power Room, bottom-right near O'aka)
- Boss: Sin's Fin + Sinspawn Echuilles (HP: ~2,000)
- O'aka: First donation opportunity

## 5. Kilika

### Kilika Port
- Ether (in hut, only if you saved child from crumbling hut)
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. IV** (inn/hut where Tidus wakes up)
- 3 Potions (NPCs)

### Kilika Woods
- 2 Mana Spheres (end of eastern pathway)
- Scout (blitzball item for Wakka, hidden pathway)
- Remedy (from guard leader)
- Luck Sphere (north on central road)
- Hi-Potion (from female Crusader)
- Elixir (speak to Luzzu after defeating Lord Ochu)
- Boss (optional): Lord Ochu (HP: ~4,649)

### Kilika Temple Cloister
- **Red Armlet** (Destruction Sphere treasure — MISSABLE, can't return)
  - Fire/Ice/Lightning Ward for Kimahri

### Boss: Sinspawn Geneaux (HP: 3,000 + tentacles)

## 6. S.S. Winno
- Hi-Potion (back of room chest)
- Hi-Potion (chest behind Besaid Aurochs)
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. V** (bridge, steering room)

## 7. Luca

### Dock Area
- 600 Gil (dock chest)
- Tidal Spear for Kimahri (dock chest)
- Phoenix Down x2 (near S.S. Winno)
- Magic Sphere + HP Sphere (hidden area at Dock 5, squeeze through crates)

### Stadium/City
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. VI** (Stadium Basement B locker room, with 2 Hi-Potions)
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. VII** (Sphere Theater lobby, floor near stairway)

### Boss: Oblitzerator (HP: 6,000)
- Use crane 3 times with Lulu's Thunder to weaken

## 8. Mi'ihen Highroad

### South/Central
- Remedy (north end chest)
- Red Ring for Yuna (talk to running man)
- 3 Softs (talk to kid, DON'T kick blitzball)
- 2 Antidotes (travelers)
- Hunter's Spear for Kimahri (traveler)
- Ice Brand for Tidus (behind ruined building on left)
- Lv. 1 Key Sphere (walking man)
- 2,000 Gil (opening on right side)
- 4 Antidotes (NPC near above)
- 600 Gil (NPC walking back and forth)

### Agency/Newroad
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. VIII** (given by Rin at Agency, unmissable) + 2 Mega-Potions
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. IX** (Newroad, north cliff-side)
- O'aka: Donate opportunity (cumulative 10,001 for discount)
- Mars Crest: Mi'ihen Oldroad South (for Auron's Masamune, accessible later)

### Boss: Chocobo Eater (HP: 10,000) — weak to Fire, push off cliff for best outcome
### Boss: Sinspawn Gui Round 1 (HP: 12,000)

## 13. Thunder Plains
- Phoenix Down x2, Hi-Potion x2, 5,000 Gil (south section, left side chests)
- Water Ball (L-shaped indentation on right)

## 14. Macalania (Woods + Lake + Temple)

### Macalania Woods

### Lake Macalania
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XV** (outside Travel Agency, left of door)

### Bosses:
- Spherimorph (HP: 12,000) — rotates elemental weakness
- Crawler (HP: ~16,000) + Negator (HP: 1,000) — destroy Negator first to use magic
- Seymour + Anima + Guado Guards (Seymour HP: 6,000)

## 21. Mt. Gagazet
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XXV** (Mountain Trail, behind rock pillar on right)
- Lv. 1 Key Sphere (after First Trial)
- Fortune Sphere (underwater after Second Trial)
- Return Sphere, Recovery Ring (chests)
- Pep Talk armor for Wakka (crevasse on right)
- Saturn Crest for Kimahri (behind pillar in Prominence area)
- **IMPORTANT: Talk to Wantz on Mountain Trail!** (enables his shop later with 4-slot equipment)
- Steal Lv. 3 Key Spheres from Biran/Yenke (4-5 if possible)

### Bosses:
- Biran & Yenke Ronso (HP scales with Kimahri) — Kimahri solo, learn Blue Magic!
- Seymour Flux (HP: ~70,000) + Mortiorchis
- Sanctuary Keeper (HP: 40,000)

- Boss: Omega Weapon (HP: 999,999)

- Venus Sigil for Lulu (200 consecutive dodges)
