## Boss Stat Blocks

### Sin (Deck Battle)

- **Sin HP:** 2,000 (Overkill: 1,000)
- **Sin AP:** 10 (Overkill: 15)
- **Sin Weakness:** None
- **Steal:** N/A
- **Sinscales HP:** 200 (Overkill: 400)
- **Sinscales AP:** 2 (Overkill: 3)
- **Sinscales Weakness:** None

**Mechanics:**
- Sin launches Sinscales onto the deck. New Sinscales replace each set of three eliminated.
- Sin's fin is the real target. It can only be damaged by Wakka's blitzball, Lulu's spells, or Yuna's aeon.
- While Wakka/Lulu/Yuna target the fin, have Tidus or Kimahri handle Sinscales.
- Leaving one Sinscale alive prevents Sin from producing more, capping incoming damage — useful to let you go all-in on the fin.
- The battle is easy enough that farming all Sinscales for AP is also a valid approach.

### Sinspawn Echuilles (Underwater)

- **HP:** Not listed in a separate stat block on these pages (stat block header on page 30 covers Sin/Sinscales; Echuilles HP is not explicitly given here)
- **AP:** Not broken out separately on these pages
- **Steal:** N/A
- **Weakness:** Listed in full in the Kilika chapter enemy header (page 30 boundary)

**Available party:** Tidus and Wakka only (Wakka dived overboard to rescue Tidus; they fight Echuilles underwater together).

**Mechanics:**
- Open with Tidus's Cheer ability to boost both men's Strength and Defense.
- Use Wakka's Dark Attack to blind Echuilles. This causes Drain Touch to miss and gives a chance to dodge the powerful Blender attack.
- Ignore the Sinscales entirely even though they continuously deal damage; focus all attacks on Echuilles to end the battle as fast as possible.
- Reapply Wakka's Dark Attack when the blind wears off.
- If Wakka runs out of MP, use an Ether to restore it.
- Watch the CTB Window on the right side of the screen closely. Heal with Potion or Hi-Potion when a character is low on HP and enemies have consecutive turns upcoming.

## O'aka Investment Table

O'aka XXIII appears below deck next to the Save Sphere. He asks for a loan — this is the first opportunity to invest. The cumulative total across all encounters determines his discount tier.

| Investment Total | Price Modifier |
|---|---|
| 0–100 Gil | Standard Price x 2.0 |
| 101–1,000 Gil | Standard Price x 1.5 |
| 1,001–10,000 Gil | Standard Price x 1.2 |
| 10,001 Gil and up | Standard Price x 0.7 (30% discount) |

**Guide advice:** Money is tight early. Don't feel obligated to give much here — there are more opportunities to add to the loan total before O'aka sells anything worth buying. The 10,001 Gil tier yields 30% off, but only pays back if you buy a lot of expensive equipment later. You'll have more investment opportunities before he starts selling worthwhile goods.

## Walkthrough Prose

### Below Deck

After speaking to Wakka, go below deck via the entrance at the rear of the ship, next to the bridge's door. In the hall, find the Save Sphere and O'aka XXIII (who asks for a loan — see investment table). Search the southeast corner of the power room (south of the stairs) to find Al Bhed Primer vol. III — only a corner of it is sticking out, easy to miss. In the cabin to the west, kick the luggage to get a Potion (repeatable until you hold 20 total). A chest across from the suitcase holds a Remedy. When finished, return to the deck and join the small crowd gathered around the summoner. Speak to Wakka again, then approach Yuna on the bow.

### Sin Deck Battle

Sin sends Sinscales to engage the party on deck. New Sinscales appear each time three are eliminated. The real target is Sin's fin, which can only be hit by Wakka's blitzball, Lulu's spells, or Yuna's aeon. Assign Tidus and Kimahri to handle Sinscales while the others focus the fin. Leaving one Sinscale alive stops Sin from producing more and limits party damage. The battle is easy — farming Sinscales for AP is also reasonable.

### Sinspawn Echuilles (Underwater)

After Wakka dives overboard to rescue Tidus, Sinspawn Echuilles emerges for an underwater battle. Only Tidus and Wakka are available. Lead with Tidus's Cheer (improves both men's Strength and Defense), then apply Wakka's Dark Attack to blind Echuilles. Blindness causes Drain Touch to miss and improves odds of dodging Blender. Ignore Sinscales completely — focus entirely on Echuilles. Reapply Dark Attack as needed; use an Ether if Wakka's MP runs dry. Monitor the CTB Window carefully and heal with Potion or Hi-Potion before enemy consecutive turns.

## Tips

- The Potion luggage kick is repeatable up to 20 Potions total — worth doing before the Sin fight.
- Al Bhed Primer vol. III is very easy to walk past; only a corner is visible in the power room corner.
- Save at the below-deck Save Sphere before returning to deck — the Sin encounter triggers once you approach Yuna at the bow.
- Wakka's Dark Attack is the decisive mechanic for Echuilles. Prioritize inflicting blind over dealing damage.
- If Wakka's MP depletes during Echuilles, use an Ether immediately rather than waiting.

## Missable Warnings

- **Al Bhed Primer vol. III** — The ship is not truly missable (you can backtrack), but the primer is extremely easy to overlook in normal play since only a corner is visible in the power room. Flag in the UI as "easy to miss — search the southeast corner of the power room."
- **Besaid Docks gifts** — Talk to all well-wishers and the boy on the plank before boarding. These are missable once you board the ship and the scene transitions. Items: Ether x1, Phoenix Down x3, Seeker's Ring x1, 400 gil, Remedy x1.
