## Boss / Enemy Notes

### Zu
- Encountered heading north from the starting oasis (described as "a massive desert scavenger")
- No HP/AP stat block given in the guide for this chapter
- Strategy:
  - Cast **Slow** on Zu, **Haste** on Tidus
  - Use **Delay Attack** abilities to further reduce Zu's number of turns
  - Use a **Darktouch** weapon to blind it if available
  - When **Auron** joins: use **Power Break**
  - When **Lulu** joins: use **Bio** to poison it

### Sandragora
- Found in sand pits in the northern desert region — guard the most valuable chests
- No HP/AP stat block given in the guide for this chapter
- Strategy:
  - Use **fire magic** and **fire weapons**
  - Cure **Confusion** immediately with **Remedies**
  - Rikku can **steal 2 Remedies** from each Sandragora if supplies are low

### Sand Worm
- Roams Sanubia Desert
- Rikku can steal **Shadow Gems** from them (useful items)
- No dedicated strategy section; mentioned in the "Yuna's Replacement" sidebar

### Cactuar
- Tutorial sphere near Wakka covers Zus and Cactuars
- **Cactuar stone** overlooking a sandstorm-swept region on the east fork: cannot interact now — relevant on a later visit (Cactuar sidequest post-airship)

## Walkthrough Prose

**Starting point:** Tidus wakes alone in a small oasis in the center of Sanubia Desert.

- Before climbing out of the oasis, swim to the **lower-left side** to find a sunken chest with **4 Remedies**.
- Head north — encounter the Zu. See enemy notes above.
- Auron joins as you head north. Continue north to find **Wakka**.
- Open the Al Bhed first aid kit near Wakka: **8 Al Bhed Potions**.
- Nearby sphere monitor has Zu and Cactuar combat tutorials.
- Al Bhed marker written in Al Bhed script. With all 16 Primers collected so far, you can read most signs. The markers are navigation aids throughout the desert.

**Reuniting with Kimahri and Rikku:**
- Head **east** from an Al Bhed marker to find **Kimahri**.
- Follow the path **west then north** to find **Rikku** at her shanty.
  - Chest at shanty: **2 Ethers**
  - First aid kit at shanty: **8 Al Bhed Potions**
- Follow Rikku through the dunes to the "Home Ahead" marker.
- From that sign, travel **southwest** to find a chest with **4 Hi-Potions**.
- In the expansive area ahead: watch the minimap for large gray spots marking desert debris — treasure chests are hidden behind them.

**Rikku's role without Yuna:**
- Al Bhed Potions heal the entire party at once in battle
- Steal Shadow Gems from Sand Worms
- Instantly KO machina enemies with her steal ability
- Keep Rikku on the front line throughout Bikanel

**The fork:**
- Al Bhed sign warns: weaker fiends to the right, stronger fiends to the left. Both paths have items — explore both.
- **East fork first:** collect **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XVIII**, then double back
- **West fork:** open 3 treasure chests
- **Inside the nearest building (west fork):** **Lv. 2 Key Sphere** and **10,000 gil**
- **Building to the north:** **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XVII**
- **Western corner of the map:** **Elixir** (final chest on the fork)
- The left (west) fork is closer to the north exit. If taking the right fork, note the Cactuar stone — cannot interact with it now.

**Northern sand pit region:**
- Travel north along the **western wall**
- Treasure chests contain: **Megalixirs**, **Teleport Spheres**
- Some chests are at the rim or bottom of sand pits — follow the swirling sands in to reach them
- **Mercury Crest** is in a small area branching off to the **west** (most valuable treasure in this zone)
- Sand pits contain Sandragora fiends — see enemy notes above

## Tips

- **Darktouch weapon** is useful against the Zu for inflicting blind.
- Keep **Rikku on the front line** — she replaces Yuna's healing role with Al Bhed Potions (party-wide cure) and can steal from fiends including Sand Worms and Sandragoras.
- With 16 Primers collected before arriving, you can read most Al Bhed signs in the desert — use them to navigate.
- The **Cactuar stone** is a placeholder for a later post-airship sidequest — no action needed on this visit.
- The **Mercury Crest** is required for Tidus's Celestial Weapon (Caladbolg). Do not skip the western branch inside the sand pit area.
- The guide notes you can't interact with the Cactuar stone now, but to remember its location for a return visit.

## Missable Warnings

- **MISSABLE — Mercury Crest**: Located in a small area that branches off to the **west** inside the sand pit region in the northernmost part of the map. Easy to walk past without following the branch. Required for Tidus's Celestial Weapon (Caladbolg) — permanently missable if the desert is not revisited.
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XVII**: Inside a specific building in the northern area (west fork). Do not leave the desert without collecting it. (Missable — this desert is not freely revisited once Home is triggered.)
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XVIII**: East fork of the split path. Collect before backtracking to the west fork. Same missability window as Vol. XVII.
