## Boss: Evrae

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 32,000 (Overkill: 2,000) |
| AP | 5,400 (Overkill: 8,100) |
| Weakness | None |
| Elemental resistances | Resistant to all elemental types |
| Steal | Water Gem |
| Drop | (not listed) |

**Special mechanics:**
- Fight takes place on the airship's external deck; Tidus can use the **Trigger Command** to communicate with Cid.
- Cid can fire a powerful missile volley when the ship is at **long range** — can be used **up to 3 times**.
- Evrae uses **Swooping Scythe** to close distance (moves from long range back to close range).
- Later in the fight, Evrae casts **Haste on itself** to counter Tidus's Slow — re-apply Slow immediately.

**Strategy:**
- Cast **Slow** on Evrae, then **Haste** on Tidus and key characters to maximize turns.
- Watch the turn order on the right side of the screen. When Tidus or Rikku's turn comes before Cid's and Evrae won't act in between: use Trigger Command to pull the ship away (long range).
- When Evrae is at long range and Cid's turn arrives, Cid fires missiles.
- **Close range** (Evrae is near the ship): use physical attackers — Tidus, Auron, Rikku.
- **Long range** (Evrae is far): have Lulu and Wakka attack; Tidus casts buff spells or Rikku uses Al Bhed Potions or other items.
- **Do not use elemental magic** — Evrae resists all elements; use non-elemental weapons.

## Bevelle Temple — Enemy Waves (Crash the Wedding)

The party must fight through several waves of Bevelle guards after landing on the rooftop. **Heal everyone before proceeding.**

**Wave 1 — Warrior Monks (standard):** Clear to advance.

**Key threat — Warrior Monks with flamethrowers:** Can douse the entire party with fire. **Always eliminate flamethrower monks first.**

**Wave 2 — Two Warrior Monks + giant machina:**
- Blind the machina with **Dark Attack** or a **Smoke Bomb**
- Quickly dispose of the Warrior Monks

**Final wave — YAT-99 (machina):**
- Defeat the guards in front of the YAT-99 first to advance on the machina
- This is the last battle before the Seymour Natus confrontation (which happens in a cutscene)

**Enemies listed in page 56 sidebar:** Warrior Monk, Swamp Mafdet, Pterya, Seymour Natus

## Cloister of Trials — Step-by-Step Solution

The Bevelle Cloister uses gliding paths. A pedestal holding a **Bevelle Sphere** transforms into a moving platform when pushed onto the path. Pressing the action button at glyphs changes direction.

**Key puzzle items:** Bevelle Sphere (need 2 at one critical point), Glyph Sphere, Destruction Sphere.

1. Ride down to the first stopping point and press the action button to begin.
2. Watch the first glyph: when the arrow points **right** and the platform is on it, press the button to turn right. Follow to the bottom.
3. Take the **Bevelle Sphere** from the bottom; insert it into the pedestal. Push pedestal back onto the path; ride back to the top.
4. At the top: use the floor glyph to turn **right**. At the next glyph, turn **right** again.
5. Remove one Bevelle Sphere from the pedestal; place it into the slot. This activates part of the lower path.
6. Push the pedestal back onto the path. At the end of the top path, use the last glyph to turn 180 degrees and go back.
7. Push the pedestal back onto the path and ride off the end to restart at the beginning of the lower path.
8. Take the **Destruction Sphere**; get back on the platform. Ride off the end to start over from the beginning.
9. Use the middle glyph to turn **right**.
10. Remove the Bevelle Sphere from the slot; place it into the pedestal. Push pedestal back onto the path; ride off the end to restart.
11. Ride the lower path to the second-to-last glyph and turn **right**.
12. Ride to the second-to-last glyph again and turn **right**.
13. Run across the long stretch; insert the **Destruction Sphere** into the slot where you got the Glyph Sphere. This opens a new path in the next corridor.
14. At the first glyph on the top path, turn **right** and ride down to the lower path.
15. Turn **left** at the intersection; go toward the end of the path.
16. Run to the end of the long path; take the **Glyph Sphere**. Place it into the pedestal; get back on the path.
17. Ride the platform back to the last glyph on the lower level; turn **right**.
18. Take the Bevelle Sphere you inserted earlier; insert it into the pedestal.
    - **CRITICAL:** If you do not have **two Bevelle Spheres** at this point, you will miss a great item (the Knight Lance)!
19. Move past the first two glyphs; turn **right** at the last glyph near the edge.
20. Take one Bevelle Sphere from the pedestal; set it into the slot to the left. This opens the path to a slot with a purple glyph.
21. Ride the lower path to the end; turn right.
22. Cross to the purple glyph; insert the Glyph Sphere. The wall gives way, revealing a Destruction Sphere.
23. Ride the platform off the end of the lower path to restart from the beginning.
24. At the **second glyph** (one not previously used), turn **right**.
25. Push the pedestal across the platform; ride to the end where the new path lies — takes Tidus up to a new area.
26. Leave the pedestal at the bottom of the stairs; ascend.
27. Open the treasure chest on the right: collect **HP Sphere**. The chest disappears, revealing a floor switch.
28. Step on the floor switch to teleport the pedestal up the stairs.
29. Take one Bevelle Sphere from the pedestal; insert it into the slot to open a path on the left.
30. Push the pedestal onto the left path; ride it to the end: receive the **Knight Lance**.
31. Ride the platform back to the top of the stairs; exit to the right.
32. Yuna receives the aeon **Bahamut**. The party is then sentenced to the **Via Purifico**.

## Via Purifico — Yuna's Route (start of next chapter, page 58 partial)

- Control Yuna; move east out of the starting chamber, follow the corridor.
- Three party members (Kimahri, Auron, Lulu) are in the maze; find them via teleport pads or wandering.
- Yuna is far behind other characters in development — consider avoiding teleport pads to give her AP.
- Bahamut's **Impulse** ability can crush any foes encountered.
- **Kimahri:** Follow corridor east then north into a room with Kimahri. After he rejoins, move left: chest with **Mega-Potion**.
- **Auron:** Continue north from Kimahri's location; winding corridor leads west to Auron. Use the Save Sphere; continue west. Go straight through the T-intersection to a locked room with treasure chests (party can return later). Head south.
- **Lulu:** Follow corridor to another teleport pad; turn onto the short eastern path. Chest beside Lulu: **White Magic Sphere**.

## Walkthrough Prose Summary

**Evrae (airship external deck):**
- Cast Slow on Evrae, Haste on party. Use Trigger Command strategically to position ship at long range for Cid's missiles (up to 3 times). Re-apply Slow if Evrae self-casts Haste. Use physical attackers at close range, Lulu/Wakka at long range. Do not use elemental attacks.

**Crashing the Wedding (rooftop):**
- Heal before proceeding. Fight through guard waves. Prioritize flamethrower Warrior Monks. Blind the machina in wave 2. Kill guards in front of the YAT-99 before targeting the machina. Final battle leads into the Seymour cutscene.

**Cloister of Trials:**
- After Yuna's escape, use the Save Sphere and descend. Use the machina panel at the top of the circular stairs to make descending easier. Find Al Bhed Primer Vol. XXII on the floor before the Cloister entrance. Complete the gliding-platform puzzle (see step-by-step above), ensuring you hold 2 Bevelle Spheres at step 18 to obtain the Knight Lance. Yuna receives Bahamut at the end.

## Tips

- **Heal before Bevelle rooftop** — multiple back-to-back enemy waves with no rest opportunity.
- Flamethrower Warrior Monks hit the entire party with fire — eliminate them first every time.
- Blind the machina in Wave 2 with Dark Attack or Smoke Bomb before killing the Warrior Monks.
- **Save Sphere is available before the Cloister of Trials** — use it; no saves during the trial or for a while afterward.
- The machina panel at the top of the circular stairs makes descending easier.
- **Two Bevelle Spheres are required simultaneously** at step 18 of the Cloister puzzle to obtain the Knight Lance. Losing track of sphere management locks you out of this item.
- In the Via Purifico, avoid teleport pads if you want Yuna to gain AP. Bahamut's Impulse handles all combat.
