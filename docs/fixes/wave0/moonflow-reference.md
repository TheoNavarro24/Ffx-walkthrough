## Boss: Extractor

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| HP | 4,000 (Overkill threshold: 600) |
| AP | 660 (Overkill: 990) |
| Weakness | Thunder |
| Steal | N/A |
| Absorbs | — |
| Immunity | — |
| Half Damage | — |

**Fight context**: Triggered automatically during the shoopuf crossing. Only Tidus and Wakka participate (Yuna is separated from the group). This is a machina enemy.

**Strategy**:
- Equip Tidus and Wakka with weapons that have the **Lightningstrike** ability before boarding.
- Tidus casts **Haste** on himself and Wakka immediately — this grants multiple attacks per Extractor turn.
- If the machina launches depth charges, use **Hi-Potions** to recover HP.
- With Lightningstrike + Haste the fight is straightforward.

**Reward**: Rikku is rescued and joins the party permanently. Unlocks the **All Together** trophy.

## Belgemine Rematch: Ixion

**Context**: Optional aeon battle on the south bank trail. Belgemine challenges Yuna.

**Setup**:
- Best started with Yuna and her aeons already in **Overdrive** mode.
- Belgemine summons **Ixion**.
- Counter with **Ifrit** — use **Hellfire** immediately.

**Tactics**:
- If Ixion casts Haste and chains attacks, heal Ifrit with **Fire spells**.
- If Ixion is approaching Overdrive before Ifrit, use **Boost** on Ifrit.
- If Ifrit falls, switch to **Valefor** as backup.

**Rewards (win or lose)**: **Summoner's Soul** — allows teaching abilities to aeons. Enables Cheer, Aim, Focus, and Reflex on all three aeons.
**Reward (win only)**: **2 Dragon Scales** — one aeon can learn Watera.

## Ochu Strategy

Ochu are the most dangerous standard enemies near the Moonflow:
- Claws inflict **Poison**.
- At low HP, they shift to **Ochu Dance** — inflicts Blindness, Silence, and Confusion on the entire party simultaneously.
- **Strategy**: Apply buffs early (Cheer, Haste on party) and debuffs early (Slow on Ochu) before committing to full offense. Handle them before they reach low HP.
- **Recommended team**: Kimahri, Auron, and Lulu (for fire spells).
- **Keep Yuna in reserve** — her aeons are ailment-immune and can rescue a bad situation if Ochu Dance lands.

## O'aka Notes

- O'aka is present at the **South Wharf** with inventory. If you have been donating to him throughout the journey, his prices are the only near-reasonable ones in the area. All other Moonflow merchants charge exorbitant rates.
- O'aka reappears at the **North Wharf** selling medicines only. Stock up — the Customize system becomes available soon and medicine supplies become important for adding weapon/armor abilities.

## Tips

- **Save before boarding the shoopuf** — the Extractor fight triggers immediately during the crossing.
- **Lv. 1 Key Spheres**: Two separate groups of x3 on the south bank. First is down a thin trail east of Shelinda; second is in an alcove on the northeast path. Both are easy to miss.
- **Magic Def Sphere**: Short detour west off the northern trail before the wharf. Easy to walk past.
- **5000 Gil**: Chest behind Lulu at the loading zone — collect before speaking to the shoopuf handler.
- **Rikku gear**: After she joins, purchase equipment for her at the North Wharf (O'aka) or in Guadosalam before pushing through.
- **Belgemine Summoner's Soul**: The ability to teach aeons Cheer/Aim/Focus/Reflex is worth getting here. Fight Belgemine while aeons have Overdrive stocked.

---

## Missable Warnings

- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XII**: On the platform above O'aka at the North Wharf. Must be collected before leaving the North Wharf area permanently.
- **5000 Gil chest**: Behind Lulu at the loading zone — the shoopuf departs once you speak to the handler, so collect beforehand.
- **Belgemine (Ixion)**: Optional but Summoner's Soul has permanent value for aeon builds. If skipped here, Belgemine reappears later but the window to get her rewards efficiently is limited.
