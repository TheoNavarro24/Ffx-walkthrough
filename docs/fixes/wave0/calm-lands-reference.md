## Boss Stat Blocks

### Belgemine's Shiva (Central Calm Lands)
- **HP:** Not listed
- **Strategy:** Summon Ifrit, have it heal itself with Fire spells. Straightforward win.
- **Rewards:** Power Sphere (x30) for winning; Aeon's Soul awarded regardless of win or loss.

### Defender X (Northeast Calm Lands exit)
- **HP:** Not listed on this page (strategy only)
- **Weakness:** None
- **Ineffective:** Demi, Bio (won't work); elemental spells/attacks become nullified one-by-one by Mighty Guard late in battle
- **Strategy:**
  - Party: Yuna, Tidus, Auron.
  - Tidus: cast Haste or Hastega on the party.
  - Yuna: use Protect spells.
  - Auron: Armor Break and Mental Break (other Breaks are ineffective).
  - Once everyone is hasted: Lulu can sub in for Tidus if she knows her "-ga" series spells.
  - If Yuna has Healer Overdrive Mode: focus on healing and Grand Summon.
  - Aeon usage: only use aeons when the aeon or Yuna is already in Overdrive — Defender X defeats them quickly.
  - **Late battle warning:** When Defender X uses Mighty Guard, it gains the ability to nullify each type of elemental damage once. Stop using elemental attacks at that point.

### Belgemine's Full Aeon Gauntlet (Remiem Temple — optional)
- No stat blocks listed in the guide for this section. The guide notes each aeon is "strongly enhanced" and directs players to the "Side Quests & Secrets" chapter for full details.
- **Key reward:** Defeating all aeons in the game grants the **Moon Sigil**.
- **Key unlock:** Defeating Bahamut here is required to recruit the Magus Sisters aeon.

### Yojimbo (Cavern of the Stolen Fayth — optional)
- No stat block on these pages. Guide directs to "Side Quests & Secrets" chapter.
- **Minimum gil required before attempting:** At least 200,000 gil (negotiation cost).

## Walkthrough Prose

### Strike Camp and Head West
Chapter begins in Macalania Woods. Save, head south through the crossroads past Kimahri to find Yuna — earns Heartstrings trophy. In the morning the group moves south.

- **Backtrack first:** Return to the campsite to collect the Lucid Ring from a partially hidden chest.
- Head west at the crossroads, continue southwest past a Bevelle sentry to find **Jecht's Sphere**.
- Continuing west returns to the junction of Macalania Woods and Thunder Plains (handled later).
- Head east to enter the Calm Lands.

### Entering the Calm Lands
- Head west down the incline — Maechen provides background on the region.
- At the base of the valley: **Rin's Travel Agency** — purchase useful armaments here.
- Start with the Monster Arena (southeast) before exploring further.
- Southeast corner of the central plain: two treasure chests (5,000 Gil, 10,000 Gil).
- Follow the eastern wall north to find a crack in the cliff leading to the Monster Arena.

### Monster Arena
- Agree to help the arena owner — he sells Capture-ability weapons.
- Capture is done by landing a physical killing blow with a Capture weapon.
- Buying Capture weapons for Yuna and Lulu is pointless (non-physical attackers).
- Recommended: buy for Auron (heavy hitter) and Wakka (accurate).
- Tip: Customize Auron's Beastmaster with the Piercing ability; augment Wakka's with an ailment-inflicting ability.
- **First quest:** Capture all nine monster species in the Calm Lands. Some only appear near the Al Bhed trading post; others only in the northwest region — travel the full area.
- **Reward for completing first quest:** Nirvana (Yuna's ultimate weapon chest — requires Celestial Mirror to open) + Farplane Wind (x60).
- Ongoing: capture fiends from every area of Spira; the arena owner combines them into powerful unique creatures with rare prize drops.

### Battle Belgemine — Calm Lands (Shiva fight)
- Large circular area in the center of the Calm Lands with an Al Bhed trading post and Save Sphere.
- Father Zuke encountered here (retired summoner).
- Al Bhed on the outpost's far left side has a message from Cid.
- Lv. 2 Key Sphere in a chest behind the outpost.
- Head southwest to encounter Belgemine — she uses Shiva.
- Win: 30 Power Spheres. Win or lose: Aeon's Soul.

### Chocobo Training (Northwest Calm Lands)
- Al Bhed Primer Vol. XXIII is near the cliff's edge in the extreme northwest corner — search carefully.
- Directly east of the primer's location: woman on a chocobo — speak to her to begin training.
- Complete four courses (beat target times) to earn the Chocobo License trophy and the right to borrow a chocobo at will.
- Can replay previous courses to beat time for an additional prize.

### Remiem Temple (Optional)
- Ride chocobo to the south entrance of the Calm Lands, head east to the cliff above the two treasure chests.
- While mounted, examine the chocobo feather on the ground to make the chocobo leap to a new area.
- Dismount and run up the path to Remiem Temple.
- Belgemine is inside for the full aeon challenge series.
- Al Bhed Primer Vol. XXIV and a chocobo racing explanation sphere are just west of the temple.

### Chocobo Racing (Remiem Temple)
- Chocobo is on the temple's right side.
- First race: don't worry about chests or shortcuts — just race down ramps in a spiral pattern, finishing before the champion chocobo. Winning earns the Cloudy Mirror.
- Subsequent races: hit as many chests as possible (determines prize if you win); avoid all poles (touching even one limits prize to only a Potion); chests also act as shortcuts.

### Activating the Celestial Mirror
- Can be done now (requires detour to Macalania Woods) or later after getting the airship.
- **If doing now:** In Macalania Woods, pass the Jecht's Sphere spot and speak to a woman who has lost her husband. Return to the campsite to find the husband. Return to the wife — child is also lost. Follow the bridge of rainbow light upward to find the child at a giant flower. Examine it to transform Cloudy Mirror into Celestial Mirror.
- **Note:** Leaving the Calm Lands means losing access to the waiting chocobo; you'll have a long walk to Mt. Gagazet on return. The guide suggests waiting until the airship if preferred.

### Heading to Mt. Gagazet / Defender X
- Leave the Calm Lands through the northeast exit.
- A few steps past the Save Sphere, **Defender X** attacks (unavoidable).

### Cavern of the Stolen Fayth (Optional)
- After Defender X, instead of crossing the bridge to Mt. Gagazet, go to the bridge's right side and descend the steep path into the gorge.
- **East of gorge bottom:** Rusty Sword (step one of Auron's Celestial Weapon quest).
- **West of gorge bottom:** entrance to Cavern of the Stolen Fayth.
  - Contains the secret aeon Yojimbo.
  - Also contains Al Bhed Primer Vol. XXV and other treasures.
  - Full map and Yojimbo negotiation strategies in "Side Quests & Secrets" chapter.
  - **Minimum 200,000 gil required** before attempting.

## Tips

- **Rin's Travel Agency:** Visit on arrival for useful armaments before exploring.
- **Monster Arena order:** Do this first so you can capture fiends as you explore the entire Calm Lands area rather than backtracking.
- **Capture weapons:** Don't waste gil on Capture gear for Yuna or Lulu. Auron + Wakka is the recommended pair. Customize Auron's with Piercing, Wakka's with an ailment ability.
- **Nirvana chest:** Requires Celestial Mirror to open — capture all nine Calm Lands species now, but the chest itself may have to wait.
- **Belgemine Aeon's Soul:** Awarded regardless of win or lose — no pressure to win, but winning gets Power Spheres too.
- **Celestial Mirror timing:** The guide notes it's easier to do after getting the airship to avoid losing chocobo access and a long walk back. Worth considering.
- **Defender X — Mighty Guard:** Stop using elemental attacks once Mighty Guard activates — it nullifies each element type once.
- **Yojimbo negotiation:** Need at least 200,000 gil. Full guide in Side Quests chapter.
- **Remiem chocobo race — first race:** Just win, don't worry about chests/shortcuts. Cloudy Mirror is the goal.
- **Remiem races for prizes:** Hit chests (shortcuts + better prizes), avoid poles (touching even one = only Potion prize).
