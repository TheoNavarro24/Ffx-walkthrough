## Boss Stat Blocks

### Geosgaeno
- **HP**: 32,767 (90 HP needed to trigger escape)
- **AP**: 0
- **Weakness**: None
- **Absorbs/Immune/Half**: Listed in sidebar (specific elements not readable from OCR, shown as "WEAKNESS (xT) ABSORPTION IMMUNITY HALF DAMAGE" — column headers only)
- **Steal**: N/A
- **Mechanics**:
  - Cannot be defeated at this point in the game; also cannot lose the fight.
  - Attack Geosgaeno three times; the fight then moves to its scripted conclusion.
  - Each counterattack deals only fractional HP damage — cannot kill Tidus.
  - Awards no AP or loot.

### Klikk
- **HP**: 1,500 (parenthetical: 400)
- **AP**: 5 (parenthetical: 7)
- **Weakness**: None
- **Steal**: Grenades
- **Mechanics**:
  - Encountered in the campfire chamber after Tidus dozes off. Fight solo against an agile opponent.
  - Monitor the CTB window on the right side of the screen. When Tidus has two consecutive turns and has taken over 200 HP damage, spend one turn using a Potion.
  - Scripted phase change: after dealing roughly 750 HP damage, a group blows down the door and Rikku joins the battle. At that point, all of Klikk's lost HP are restored.
  - When it's Rikku's turn: scroll to Special > Use and throw a Grenade.
  - Have Tidus keep attacking while Rikku throws Grenades. When she runs out, she can steal more directly from Klikk.

### Tros
- **HP**: Not given on pages 24–26 (stat block not visible in OCR for this chapter's pages)
- **Steal**: Not specified on these pages
- **Mechanics** (described in the "Regroup on the Ship" section, page 26):
  - After each character uses their first turn (attack or steal), Tros swims to the chamber's other side; tutorial on Trigger Commands begins.
  - Press left to access Trigger Commands and choose "Stand By" — passes your turn and restores 50 HP to each character.
  - Use Cheer on Tidus's next turn if available.
  - After the brief rest, Tros uses Nautilus Charge — attacks both characters simultaneously. Recover with Potions.
  - Throw a Grenade when you get the opportunity.
  - Tros swims away again. Press Circle until it's Tidus's turn.
  - Second round of Trigger Commands: "Pincer attack" option appears — use it to surround Tros and prevent its Nautilus Charge.

## Walkthrough Prose

### Explore the Submerged Ruins (page 24)
Tidus is alone and adrift in a sea of ruins. Use the left analog stick to guide Tidus through the water; press Circle to dive under the surface.

- **Far west**: Small stairs lead to a chunk of dry land. The blue globe is the Al Bhed Compilation Sphere (skip on first playthrough). Travel south from the sphere to find a chest with two Potions.
- **Far east**: Another platform. A sign written in Al Bhed (unreadable until the Dictionary is complete). Directly behind the sign is a partially hidden chest containing 200 Gil.
- **North rim**: Follow the red arrow on the auto-map to stairs leading to a stone bridge. There is a Save Sphere here, and a chest with a Hi-Potion (turn west when the bridge forks).
- Shortly after the fork, the bridge collapses, sending Tidus back into the pool. Swim north and fight a trio of Sahagins. After killing two, the third is consumed by the giant Geosgaeno.

### Gather Fuel for the Fire (pages 24–25)
After escaping Geosgaeno, navigate up the stairs and through the door. You need to build a fire. In the center of the massive room are logs that can be kindled. Collect all items before lighting:

1. Go through the doors near the Save Sphere to the south — search the open drawer for the Flint.
2. Head to the north part of the map and enter the doorway marked with a green square on the on-screen map. Ascend stairs and examine the dried flowers for the Withered Bouquet.
3. Don't miss the Ether chest on the way, or the Hi-Potion chest on the upper-level passage past the bouquet.
4. Back in the central chamber: look to the right of the northwest locked door for a hidden chest containing an X-Potion.
5. Save at the Save Sphere, then examine the logs to set them ablaze.

Shortly after Tidus dozes off, the campfire goes out — Klikk appears. Fight begins.

### Awaken on the Al Bhed Ship (page 25)
After the Klikk fight, Tidus is abducted and put to work on the Al Bhed ship.

- On the ship's deck: talk to the soldier on the left to receive three Potions.
- Northeast corner of the deck: pick up Al Bhed Primer Vol. I — triggers the game's first trophy.
- Return to the girl (Rikku) who speaks Tidus's language and talk to her. Tutorial on Abilities and the Sphere Grid follows. The Sphere Grid command now appears on the Main Menu.
- Tidus should have gained a Sphere Level from the Klikk fight. Use the Sphere Grid to acquire the Cheer ability.

### Join the Salvage Operation (page 25)
After speaking to Rikku a second time, she and Tidus dive to perform a salvage operation. Press Circle to dive, following the chain to the sunken ship.

- Piranhas may be encountered en route — steal Grenades from them, earn spheres, and gain AP.
- Inside the sunken ruins: Save Sphere just past the entrance. Press Circle to descend to it.
- Swim forward and examine the large control panel to open the door.
- Swim east through the next large chamber where more Piranhas attack. Have Rikku steal as many Grenades as possible; let Tidus handle the kills.
- Examine the mysterious device together — this draws the attention of the Klikk. Swim back to the first chamber to engage it.

### Regroup on the Ship (page 26 — Baaj chapter conclusion)
After the battle, follow Rikku through the hole in the wall and swim in the direction of the red arrow. The crew returns to the ship. Use the Save Sphere during dialogue. Speak to Rikku again when ready to continue.

The Tros boss fight takes place here (see stat block above for Trigger Command mechanics).

## Tips

- **Compilation Sphere**: Only useful on NG+ for importing the Al Bhed Dictionary. Ignore on a first playthrough.
- **Sphere Grid**: Acquire Cheer for Tidus as soon as he earns his first Sphere Level (should happen after the Klikk fight). It reduces incoming physical damage and is immediately useful.
- **Klikk HP reset**: Klikk's HP fully restores at the scripted phase change (around 750 damage dealt). Do not burn resources trying to finish the fight before Rikku joins — the reset will happen regardless.
- **Piranha farming**: Use Piranha encounters in the underwater ruins to stockpile Grenades for the Klikk and Tros fights via Rikku's steal.
- **Item collection order**: Guide implies collecting all items in the ruins chamber (Flint, Withered Bouquet, Ether, Hi-Potion, X-Potion) before examining the logs to start the fire.
- **Tros — Stand By**: Using the "Stand By" Trigger Command restores 50 HP to each character. Use it whenever the option is available early in the fight before Tros uses Nautilus Charge.
- **Tros — Pincer Attack**: On the second swim-away, the Pincer Attack Trigger Command becomes available. Use it immediately — it prevents Nautilus Charge.
