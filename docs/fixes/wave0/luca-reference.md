## Boss: OBLITZERATOR

- **Location**: Al Bhed ship at Dock 4 (after the party leaps aboard the departing ship)
- **HP**: 6,000 (Overkill: 600)
- **AP**: 36 (Overkill: 54)
- **Weakness**: Thunder
- **Steal**: N/A
- **Notes**: Stat block also lists Absorption / Immunity / Half Damage categories (details not specified in text)

### Strategy
- Difficulty is high for this point — Kimahri and Tidus rely on physical attacks, Lulu is casting low-level spells.
- The crane on the platform's right side is the key mechanic: cast Thunder on the crane each round to charge it. After charging it **three times**, use Tidus's Trigger Command to activate the crane. The crane rips off the top half of the Oblitzerator, greatly reducing its HP and immobilizing it.
- If Tidus has learned Haste, cast it on Lulu first so she can charge the crane faster.
- Once the crane has activated, the fight becomes manageable.
- Context: party is Lulu, Tidus, and Kimahri (Yuna is being rescued).

## Blitzball: Luca Goers Match

Not a standard combat encounter. Story continues regardless of win or loss.

### Key Info
- The Luca Goers are championship-caliber; their players' HP is nearly twice that of the Besaid Aurochs.
- Winning is possible with the right strategy.

### Strategy
- **First half**: Focus on passing between players so everyone can level up at halftime.
- **Halftime**: Equip Tidus with Jecht Shot or Sphere Shot (whichever is available).
- **First three minutes of second half**: Get the ball to Tidus so he can use his shot ability for a goal.
- **After three minutes**: Wakka replaces Tidus on the field — use his Venom Shot to attempt a goal.
- **Win reward**: Strength Sphere + Teamwork trophy. Loss yields no sphere.

## Post-Blitzball Enemies (Stadium Escape)

### Sahagin Chiefs (sphere pool)
- Tidus and Wakka fight several groups on their own.
- Each has approximately 170 HP.
- If standard attacks fall 10–20 points short of killing them outright, use Tidus's Cheer ability to push over the threshold.
- If still insufficient: use Wakka's Dark Attack to blind one enemy early, then ignore it and focus on the others.

### Vouivre
- Auron arrives dramatically and his piercing sword kills the Vouivre in one stroke.

### Garuda
- Use Wakka's Dark Attack to blind the Garuda — this causes its Sonic Boom attacks to consistently miss.
- Auron's Power Break reduces the Garuda's attack damage.

## Tips

- **Blitzball tutorial**: After the locker room scene, re-enter the Aurochs' locker room and examine the board next to Wakka to replay the tutorial. The last opportunity is the blue monitor on the wall — examine it before speaking to Wakka to start the match.
- **Luca Goers difficulty**: Despite their superior stats, the Goers can be beaten by leveraging leveling at halftime and Tidus's special shots.
- **Crane mechanic (Oblitzerator)**: Three Thunder casts on the crane (not on the boss) are required before the Trigger Command becomes available. Do not waste turns attacking the Oblitzerator directly until after the crane fires.
- **Sphere Theater**: Music and Movie Spheres are too expensive to buy at this point — just collect the Al Bhed Primer from the ground and move on.
- **O'aka**: Visit him near Dock 1 to continue investment donations and browse his stock.

## Missable Warnings

- **Al Bhed Primer vol. VI** — must be collected during the free-roam period after leaving the locker room, before progressing far into the story. Located behind the opposing player's foot in the locker room hallway.
- **Al Bhed Primer vol. VII** — on the ground at the Sphere Theater on the way out of Luca. The route south from the shop and north past the fountain is easy to skip when following Auron directly to the docks.
- **Dock 5 treasure chests** (Magic Sphere, HP Sphere) — hidden behind crates at the back of Dock 5; easy to overlook when rushing through the docks during the rescue sequence.
- **1000 gil chest** — requires climbing all the way to the top of the town square stairs, past the balcony. Easy to miss if exploration is cut short.
- **Strength Sphere** — only awarded for winning the blitzball match vs. the Luca Goers. Losing the match yields no sphere reward.
