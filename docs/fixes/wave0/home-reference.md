## Enemy Encounters

### Home — Center Area
- **Guado Guardian + mixed monsters**: Take out the Guado Guardian first — he casts support spells on other enemies. They no longer have Auto-Potion, so no need to steal before attacking.

### Home — After Cid's Warning
- **Guado Guardian + Dual Horns**: Second wave after the party exits the center area.

## Walkthrough Prose

**Outside Home:**
- Search the dead body near the entrance: **2 Hi-Potions**
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XIX** is nearby — collect immediately
- After the party congregates in the center area, Guado Guardian + monsters attack. Eliminate the Guado Guardian first (casts support spells on allies)
- Two treasure chests in this room have Al Bhed text — requires Primers to unlock
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XX** is on the bed nearby — collect it
- With all volumes collected to this point, the chests should be solvable

**After first wave:**
- Follow the party out of the center area
- Cid warns the group; second attack wave: Guado Guardian + Dual Horns

**Secret Stash:**
- Party automatically descends stairs into Home's central hub
- Turn around, head south; look for green dot on map (openable door to the left)
- Don't miss the **Al Bhed Potion chest** obscured by smoke on the way
- Right chest: Al Bhed puzzle — answer correctly to get **Friend Sphere** (see solution above)
- Left chest: "Chest of Dreams" — choose your preferred item or monster

**Airship — Northeast Corridor:**
- Exit the living quarters, descend the stairs
- Go straight up the corridor, past the intersection; chest with **6 Al Bhed Potions**
- In the northeast corridor: **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XXI** on the floor
- Enter the blue-lit door nearby; two more Al Bhed puzzle chests:
  - Number-locked chest: **Special Sphere** (use the calculation table above)
  - Question-answer chest: **Skill Sphere** (see answer table above)

**Save the Captives:**
- As you head through the crumbling city section, search **under the stairs** for a **Lv. 4 Key Sphere**
- Before entering the Summoner's Sanctum, don't miss the **Lv. 2 Key Sphere** on the **far right**
- Inside the sanctum: chest with **10,000 gil**
- Head for the airship dock

**Console the Home Refugees:**
- Talk to everyone on the bridge, then travel south to leave
- Pass Kimahri and Isaaru in the hallway; continue to higher decks
- **Rin** is available in the hallway — selling arms and items (shop opportunity)
- On the next highest level, go through the door — **Dona** is here; your answer to her affects her future appearances in the game
- Explore the ship's decks for other Al Bhed survivors; Al Bhed Psyches are on the lowest deck to the north — recruitable for Blitzball but likely already under contract
- Return to the bridge and talk to the pilot (man behind Cid) to determine Yuna's location
- When trying to leave, Guado fiends have boarded the ship — fight your way to the cabin
- One refugee gives **4 Al Bhed Potions** along the way
- **Save at the final Save Sphere** before taking the elevator to the external deck — boss fight ahead and no save opportunity for a while after

## Tips

- Eliminate the Guado Guardian before the monsters in the center area — he buffs enemies.
- The Al Bhed puzzle chests require at least Primers I–XX to solve. Collect all Primers as soon as they appear.
- Rin has a shop on the airship — stock up on items before the Evrae boss fight.
- **Save before the elevator to the external deck** — Evrae boss follows and there is no save point after until much later.
- Dona's dialogue choice here has story/appearance consequences — choose based on preferred outcome.

## Missable Warnings

- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XIX** — Near the dead body outside Home. This entire area is only accessible during this chapter. **Permanently missable after leaving.**
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XX** — On the bed in the center area of Home. **Permanently missable after leaving.**
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XXI** — On the floor of the northeast airship corridor. **Permanently missable after this sequence.**
- **Lv. 2 Key Sphere** — Far right before the Summoner's Sanctum entrance. Easy to overlook in the chaos.
- **Lv. 4 Key Sphere** — Under the stairs in the crumbling city. Check under all stairs.
- **Al Bhed Potion chest** — Obscured by smoke in the stash corridor; do not run past it.
- **Friend Sphere** (right puzzle chest) and **items from Chest of Dreams** (left puzzle chest) — only accessible during this visit to Home.
