## Boss Stat Blocks

### Kimahri
- **HP**: 750 (300 in parentheses — Overkill threshold)
- **AP**: 3 (4 Overkill)
- **Weakness**: None
- **Steal**: N/A
- **Absorption / Immunity**: None listed
- **Location**: Mountain path north of Besaid, at an ancient structure
- **Attacks**: Physical attacks + Jump attack (alternates)
- **Strategy**:
  - Use Cheer ability to reduce damage from all of Kimahri's attacks.
  - Keep attacking; do not waste healing items — Tidus's HP and MP are fully restored after the fight.
  - If Tidus's Overdrive triggers, use it to end the fight early.

## Walkthrough Prose

**Meet the Besaid Aurochs** (page 27)
Tidus meets the Besaid Aurochs blitzball team on the beach. Collect items around the beach area:
- Do not miss the Moon Crest in a small cove just east of where you first met the team.
- Two Antidotes are in a chest to the west.
- Talk repeatedly to all Aurochs members to collect Potions, a Hi-Potion, and ~200 Gil.

Follow Wakka inland to a high cliff over a beautiful lagoon. Wakka shoves Tidus into the water. Swim and dive to collect items from the three sunken treasure chests along the southeast wall. Piranhas are in the water — follow the red auto-map arrow to dry land without lingering.

**Visit Wakka's Village** (page 27)
Head down the mountain from the Save Sphere toward the village. Wakka and Tidus encounter two Crusaders. After Wakka teaches Tidus the prayer of Yevon, explore the village:

- Outside the first tent to the west: Phoenix Down.
- Inside that tent: item shop selling Potions, Phoenix Downs, and Antidotes.
- North of that tent: tall grass hides a western passage leading to three chests — Hi-Potion, 400 Gil, two Potions.
- Northwest tent (Crusaders Lodge): Speak to Gatta and Luzzu about the origin of the Crusaders and their war with Sin.
- Inn: Optional rest stop. Sphere monitor next to the attendant's desk lets you review past tutorials.
- Center east tent: Wakka is waiting, but wants Tidus to visit the temple first.

**Pay Your Respects at the Temple** (page 27)
After speaking with the Crusaders, run to the temple on the hilltop. Inside, head toward the central stairs. After a lecture about summoners and the principles of Yevon, return to Wakka's hut and accept his offer to nap. When Tidus wakes up, return to the temple and move to Wakka's side. Worried about the apprentice summoner Yuna, Tidus pushes his way into the inner sanctum — triggering the Cloister of Trials puzzle.

**Unlock the Cloister of Trials** (page 28)
First Cloister of Trials in the game. These puzzle areas appear in every temple throughout Spira. Use magical glyphs and spheres to open the path to the Chamber of the Fayth. Powerful treasures are hidden here, accessible only with Destruction Spheres.

Step-by-step solution:
1. Move to the back of the first area; examine the glyph on the wall to open a new path.
2. Examine the glyph on the right side of the room to reveal a secret chamber.
3. Take the **Glyph Sphere** from the open door panel; proceed to the next sphere recess.
4. Examine the runes across from the pedestal to reveal a Glyph; examine it again to open a hidden chamber.
5. Enter the chamber and remove the **Besaid Sphere**.
6. Leave the pedestal and return toward the Destruction Sphere.
7. Go to the new opening in the wall — take the **Rod of Wisdom** from the chest inside.
8. Descend the stairs to the small overhang on the wall. Examine it (brief sphere explanation), then obtain the **Glyph Sphere**.
9. Move to the door at the bottom of the stairs; insert the Glyph Sphere to open it.
10. Insert the Glyph Sphere to reveal a secret room. Inside is a **Destruction Sphere** — do not take it yet. Proceed to the next area.
11. Insert the **Besaid Sphere** into the pedestal. This reveals another chamber; Tidus pushes the pedestal inside.
12. Place the **Destruction Sphere** in the slot where the Besaid Sphere was. This opens a hidden chamber on the far side of the Cloister.
13. Return to the pedestal and push it into the room until it locks into place and sinks into the floor.

**Prepare for Yuna's Pilgrimage** (page 28)
Follow Wakka's group out of the temple to the village circle. Yuna summons Valefor for the first time. That night around the campfire, talk to Yuna. Speak to Wakka and choose to sleep.

During the night, Tidus has a strange dream. Move him toward Yuna on the pier.

The next morning, Wakka gives Tidus **Brotherhood**. After Yuna joins the party, the group leaves the village. Tidus equips Brotherhood automatically; **manually equip Yuna with the Rod of Wisdom** from the temple.

**MISSABLE — Al Bhed Primer Vol. II and Valefor's second Overdrive**: Before going far, turn around and return to Besaid:
- Pick up **Al Bhed Primer Vol. II** from the ground near the lodge's reception desk.
- Speak to the item shop owner about a strange discovery made by her dog. If the dog is not in the shop, search the town. Talk to the dog — it gives you the item it found. This unlocks **Valefor's second Overdrive attack** (Energy Blast / more powerful).

**Take the First Steps of Your Journey** (page 28)
As the party ascends the mountain, a series of tutorial battles teaches each character's special skills. At the top of the mountain, Tidus can optionally pray with the others. Head north from the prayer location toward an ancient structure. An Al Bhed message on the first column is unreadable for now. Continue along the path — Kimahri jumps out and attacks Tidus. [See boss block above.]

**Embark at the Besaid Docks** (page 29 — last Besaid section)
After the Kimahri battle, follow the mountain trail past the waterfalls. Additional tutorial battles ahead. In one fight, switch out Tidus for Yuna; summon Valefor and cast magic on the enemy. Watch for equipment drops in this area: Tidus's Metal Shield, Lulu's Bright Bangle and Variable Mog.

Return to the beach where you first met Wakka and follow the party to the pier. Before boarding, talk to the well-wishers to receive:
- Ether x1
- Phoenix Down x3 ("three tufts")
- Seeker's Ring x1
- 400 Gil

Talk to the little boy on the boarding plank to receive a **Remedy**, then board the S.S. Liki.

## Tips

- **Valefor's second Overdrive (Energy Blast)**: Talk to the item shop owner about her dog, then find and talk to the dog in the village. This is only available before departing Besaid. Significant power upgrade — do not skip.
- **Rod of Wisdom**: Found in the Cloister chest; must be **manually equipped to Yuna** — not auto-equipped.
- **Brotherhood**: Auto-equipped to Tidus when Wakka gives it the morning after the campfire.
- **Kimahri fight**: Use Cheer to reduce incoming damage. Tidus's HP and MP are restored to full after the fight — do not waste Potions.
- **Cloister Destruction Sphere**: Unlocks the Rod of Wisdom chest. Do not skip step 12 in the puzzle or you'll miss it.
- **Piranhas in the lagoon**: Minor threat; move quickly to collect the three sunken chests and exit the water.
- **Item shop**: Stocks Potions, Phoenix Downs, and Antidotes. Stock up before leaving Besaid.
- **Sphere monitor at inn**: Lets you review past tutorials.
- **Equipment drops on mountain path**: Tidus's Metal Shield, Lulu's Bright Bangle and Variable Mog can drop from enemies — equip immediately if obtained.
