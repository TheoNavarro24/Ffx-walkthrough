## 4. Walkthrough Prose

### Race to the Stadium
Tidus is heading to a blitzball match at Zanarkand's stadium. A small crowd has gathered outside his residence. Speak with the two women and the group of children in the front row to advance through the crowd. Run down a bridge full of fans while a commentator provides backstory, then fight through the mob to the stadium entrance.

While on the bridge, repeatedly talk to the wandering woman with yellow leggings and a pink top. Tell her you can get her a ticket — she rewards you with two Potions.

### Survive Sin's Assault
Tidus's blitzball match is cut short when Sin attacks. He encounters Auron, who introduces Sin and gives Tidus a Longsword. Use the Attack command to cut through the first two waves of Sinscales. In the second wave, attack only enemies directly in front — enemies behind are immediately replaced with reinforcements.

**Sinspawn Ammes (wave 3 — boss)**:
- Press left on the directional button to access Auron's Overdrive (Bushido). Select Dragon Fang and quickly enter the on-screen commands for maximum power.
- Tidus's Overdrive is called Swordplay. Press the button when the marker is in the center of the meter; attack strength is determined by time remaining on the clock.
- After both characters use their Overdrives, finish the sinspawn with normal attacks.

### Cut Through Sinscales
Just when Tidus and Auron think they have broken through, they are surrounded by even more Sinscales. Watch carefully for enemies whose wings start to flicker — eliminate those first or they use the damaging Spines attack. After the first wave is defeated and replaced, Auron instructs Tidus to attack the Tanker hanging from the bridge. Focus all attacks on the Tanker until it falls and explodes.

After the encounter, move to the Save Sphere beyond the sinspawn's corpse. Touching the Save Sphere restores both characters to full health.

## 6. Tips

- **Save Potions**: Demi cannot reduce HP to zero — there is no risk of dying in the Sinspawn Ammes fight. Do not waste Potions here.
- **Overdrive input timing**: Auron's Dragon Fang requires quick directional inputs on-screen; Tidus's Swordplay requires precise button timing on a moving marker. Both reward speed and accuracy.
- **Tanker target**: When surrounded after wave 1, ignore the flanking Sinscales entirely and focus all attacks on the Tanker hanging from the bridge.
- **Flickering wings = Spines incoming**: Sinscales telegraphing Spines via wing flicker must be prioritized immediately.
- No Sphere Grid advice, O'aka appearances, or mini-games in this chapter.
