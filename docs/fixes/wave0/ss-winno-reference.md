## Jecht Shot Mini-Game

The Jecht Shot is learned via a timed mini-game on deck. This is the only opportunity to learn it in the game.

**Trigger:** Examine the blitzball on the upper deck when ready to end the voyage.

**Mechanic:** Various combinations of D-pad directions and the Circle button appear as on-screen prompts (lines of dialogue). Press the D-pad in the direction shown (along with Circle) to match each prompt successfully.

**Reward:** Tidus learns the Jecht Shot blitzball move and earns the **Striker** trophy.

**Consequence of failure:** If you fail, the Jecht Shot is permanently lost. The guide explicitly states: "That was your only chance. Good thing you saved, right?" — implying you must reload a save and retry.

## Walkthrough Prose

### Boarding and Below Deck
After completing the Kilika Temple and obtaining Ifrit, travel back to town and board the waiting vessel, the S.S. Winno. Below deck: grab the Hi-Potion from the chest in your cabin, then head outside and speak to O'aka again. With more gil earned since the S.S. Liki crossing, your party is likely in a better position to lend O'aka money — this is a good moment to increase your loan.

### Deck Events
Head to the deck for optional events. Pick up Al Bhed Primer vol. V from the deckhouse near the stairs. Move to Yuna's location at the rear of the boat to interrupt a conversation between Yuna and the Luca Goers blitzball team. Speak to Yuna again afterward.

### Upper Deck Backstory
Ascend the stairs to the upper deck to overhear Lulu and Wakka talking. Only a fraction of the backstory is revealed each trip upstairs — go up and down multiple times to hear all of it.

### Jecht Shot Sequence
When ready to end the voyage: save at the Save Sphere below deck first. Then examine the blitzball on the upper deck. Follow the on-screen prompts (D-pad direction + Circle for each line of dialogue) to successfully complete the Jecht Shot attempt. Success permanently grants Tidus the Jecht Shot move and awards the Striker trophy.

## Tips

- **Save before the Jecht Shot.** This is a missable trophy-linked mini-game with no in-game retry. The guide explicitly reminds you to save first. Failure means permanently losing both the move and the Striker trophy — you must reload.
- The upper deck Lulu/Wakka conversation reveals backstory incrementally — walk up and down multiple times if you want to hear all of it.
- This is another opportunity to increase the O'aka loan; with more gil from Kilika, a higher investment is more feasible here than on the S.S. Liki.
- Pick up Al Bhed Primer vol. V from the deckhouse before triggering departure (examining the blitzball). The ship content can all be done in one pass.

---

## Missable Warnings

- **Jecht Shot (and Striker trophy)** — MISSABLE. Examining the blitzball and failing the mini-game means permanently losing the Jecht Shot move and the associated Striker trophy. Save at the below-deck Save Sphere before attempting. If you fail, reload and retry.
- **Al Bhed Primer vol. V** — Technically not permanently missable (it is available for the duration of the voyage), but must be collected from the deckhouse before triggering departure. Pick it up before examining the blitzball.
