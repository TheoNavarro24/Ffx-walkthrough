## Boss / Key Enemy Notes

### Basilisk (field enemy, not a scripted boss)
- Inflicts Petrify via gaze attack.
- Use Kimahri's **Lancet** on a Basilisk to learn **Stone Breath** — one of his most powerful Overdrives.
- Equip Yuna with a **Soft Ring** so she is petrify-immune and can revive the rest of the party.

*No scripted boss fight in this chapter.*

## Cloister of Trials — Step-by-Step

The Djose Cloister uses electrically charged Djose Spheres to power pedestals and activate floor/ceiling symbols.

1. Take the Djose Spheres from the left and right wall slots; place them into the two slots on the double doors to open them.
2. In the next chamber, push the pedestal holding the Djose Sphere under the upside-down pedestal — this super-charges the sphere.
3. Push the pedestal (now holding two Djose Spheres) into the lightning-filled room — it becomes a suspended platform. Leap across.
4. Take the super-charged sphere from the slot right of the door; place it in the slot left of the door.
5. Find the glowing spot on the west wall near the floor switch — examine it to reveal a glyph.
6. Insert the super-charged sphere into the slot right of the central door; this opens a room with a lightning pit.
7. Push the pedestal on the other side of the lightning pit — activates the "iris" of the floor symbol. The pedestal becomes an elevator.
8. Ride the elevator up. Touch the glyph on the wall to reveal the **Destruction Sphere**.
9. Take the Destruction Sphere; ride the magic lift back down.
10. Remove both Djose Spheres from the eastern wall and place them on the pedestal.
11. Jump back across the pedestal platform to the main room.
12. Use the west-side floor switch to extract the pedestal from the lightning.
13. On the upper level are five pedestals with Djose Spheres. Press Circle (©) at each to push it into the wall, activating one ceiling spoke. All five must be activated.
14. Place the **Destruction Sphere** into the central pedestal — destroys a section of the east wall, revealing a chest containing the **Magic Sphere**.
15. Ascend the west stairs. Take a Djose Sphere from either side of the door; walk to the eastern platform and place it into one of the two slots.
16. Take the second Djose Sphere and place it into the remaining slot.
17. Step on the far-west floor button to move the pedestal back to center.
18. Take the Djose Spheres from each side of the pedestal.
19. Return to the first chamber and place the Djose Spheres into the slots on each side of the room — activates the two "wings" of the floor symbol.
20. With all five ceiling spokes active, a stairway appears on the west side of the room and a new pedestal appears in the center.
21. Ride the elevator back down to exit.

## Walkthrough Prose — Key Points

**Djose Highroad:**
- All of the soldiers praying to Yevon will give useful items and weapons; a priest contributes Hi-Potions.
- The first chest contains two Phoenix Downs. Directly across the path, behind a spiky rock, is Al Bhed Primer Vol. XI.
- Further north, a small alcove on the west side holds the Bright Bangle.
- When the road splits, check the world map sign, then head north and speak to Auron before going to the temple.

**Temple approach:**
- After the temple opens, search far west for the 4000 Gil chest.
- East side of the temple entrance: a Crusader from Besaid. Behind the small inn to the east: Ability Sphere chest (x4).
- The chest inside the inn is blocked by a priest — return after the Cloister.

**Inside the temple:**
- Yuna meets summoner Isaaru and his guardian brothers.
- Follow the party upstairs. Left chamber exterior: Ether. Inside left chamber: Remedy. Right chamber: Mega Phoenix.
- Speak to the priest when ready to begin the Cloister trials.

**Post-Cloister — next morning:**
- Tidus wakes rested at the inn. Collect the Switch Hitter from the now-accessible inn chest.
- Speak to the party outside, then enter the temple to wake Yuna.
- On the bridges heading away from the temple: former Crusader gives a Halberd, a priest gives Hi-Potions (x2), lone man gives 10 Potions (talk repeatedly).
- After checking on the Chocobo Knights, follow the group south to the Djose Highroad split, then head north toward the Moonflow.

## Tips

- **Stone Breath timing**: Basilisks only appear on the Djose Highroad. Use Kimahri's Lancet before moving on — this is the only opportunity.
- **Soft Ring on Yuna**: Makes her immune to Petrify, allowing her to heal/revive the rest of the party safely against Basilisks.
- **Inn chest gating**: Return to the inn chest (Switch Hitter) after the Cloister before leaving the temple area.
- **Lone man on bridge**: He only gives 10 Potions after his dialogue loop completes — keep talking until he yields them.
- **Destruction Sphere reward**: The Magic Sphere hidden behind the east wall is the Cloister's secret/optional prize.

## Missable Warnings

- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XI** — hidden behind a rock on the Djose Highroad, across from the first Phoenix Down chest. Must be collected before leaving this section. The game does not return the player to the Highroad under normal story progression.
- **Stone Breath Overdrive (Kimahri)** — learned via Lancet on a Basilisk on the Djose Highroad only. Basilisks do not appear in later areas.
- **Switch Hitter (inn chest)** — accessible only after the Cloister is complete. Collect it before departing the temple area or it is forfeited.
