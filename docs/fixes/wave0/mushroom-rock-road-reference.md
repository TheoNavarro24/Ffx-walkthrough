## Boss: SINSPAWN GUI

- **Location**: Operation Mi'ihen battlefield / command center
- **HP**:
  - Head: 4,000
  - Body: 12,000
  - Arms: 800 each
- **AP**: 400 (numbers in parentheses in stat block — 800, 800, 500 — appear to be per-part AP values)
- **Weakness**: None
- **Steal**: N/A
- **Immunity / Absorption / Half Damage**: noted in stat block; elemental details not spelled out in text

### Strategy — Phase 1

- Starting party: Lulu, Wakka, and Yuna — exclusively target the **Head**.
- Occasionally the Head begins to move — counter the impending attack with any long-range attack or spell. If unable to, use Yuna's **Esuna** to clear the deadly Venom poison.
- Save mass-damage Overdrives and Yuna's summons until the Head is nearly dead, then use them to finish the Head and simultaneously damage other body parts.
- **Arms**: They protect the Body and regenerate every third turn Gui takes. Take them out before turning full attention to the Body.
- After the Head falls, bring in heavy hitters — **Auron and Kimahri** — to attack the Body.
- Continue saving aeons and Overdrives for moments when all three targets can be hit at once.

### Strategy — Phase 2 (second round)

- After a long event scene, Gui returns in a **far weaker state**.
- **Seymour** joins as a powerful guest fighter.
- **Optional**: Stall the Phase 2 fight until Seymour fills his Overdrive gauge — this is the **only opportunity in the entire game** to see Seymour's Overdrive. The guide warns it can take an extremely long time.

## Key NPC Events

### O'aka (p. 40–41)
- Appears in the western area after Clasko redirects you north — full inventory of high-priced equipment and items. Continue investing.
- Also stands to the left of the command center entrance at the top of the road with more equipment to sell.

### Shelinda (p. 40)
- Located somewhere on the road. Speaks with the party; offers **free healing** — talk to her anytime HP is low.

### Luzzu and Gatta — Conversation Before Boss (p. 40)
- Past the Save Sphere near the top, witness a conversation between Luzzu and Gatta.
- Speak to **Luzzu again** after the cutscene.
- **Save your game** at the nearby Save Sphere before riding the machina to the command center.

### Gatta Dialogue Choice — MISSABLE STORY EVENT (p. 41)
- After meeting Maester Kinoc inside the command center, speak to Gatta.
- **This dialogue determines who dies in the upcoming battle**:
  - Speak to Gatta **twice**, choosing the **second option both times** → **Luzzu dies**
  - Any other dialogue choice → **Gatta dies**
- There is no option to save both. Choose deliberately and irreversibly.

## Tips

- **Yevon platforms**: Step on the platform bearing the Yevon symbol and press Circle (©) to rise or descend. Used multiple times throughout the road to traverse cliff sections.
- **Serene Armlet vs. Primer paths**: These are two separate western detours. The Serene Armlet requires riding a Yevon platform **down**. The Primer is at the tip of a C-shaped path — do not confuse them.
- **C-shaped path**: Easy to miss — it leads west off the main route and curves. Al Bhed Primer vol. X is at the far western tip.
- **Sphere Grid**: After the battle, when Tidus awakens, the guide explicitly advises taking time to learn new abilities on the Sphere Grid before proceeding.
- **Save before the boss**: Save at the Save Sphere before riding the machina up to the command center.
- **Seymour's Overdrive**: Only visible in Phase 2 of the Sinspawn Gui fight. If you want to see it, stall before finishing Gui in Phase 2.

## Missable Warnings

- **Al Bhed Primer vol. X** — on the C-shaped western side path mid-area. Point of no return: cannot return to Mushroom Rock Road after departing for Djose Highroad. **Do not skip.**
- **Serene Armlet** — in the small area below the western Yevon platform detour. Same point of no return. **Do not skip.**
- **Gate donations** — the 100/1,000/10,000 gil donations at the Mushroom Rock gates (before Seymour arrives) yield a Scout, Ice Lance, and Moon Ring respectively. Available only before entering the road proper.
- **Gatta/Luzzu dialogue choice** — irreversible. Locked in by your dialogue selection with Gatta at the command center. No way to change the outcome afterward.
- **Seymour's Overdrive** — only viewable during Phase 2 of the Sinspawn Gui fight. Stall Phase 2 if you want to see it; the opportunity is gone once Gui is defeated.
- **All Mushroom Rock Road items** — no return after proceeding to Djose Highroad. Collect everything before triggering the final machina ascent to the command center.
