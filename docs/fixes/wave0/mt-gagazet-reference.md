## Boss Stat Blocks

### Biran & Yenke
- **HP**: Biran: Varies (overkill: 2500) / Yenke: Varies (overkill: 2500)
- **AP**: 4500 (overkill: 6750) each
- **Weakness**: None
- **Steal**: Lv. 3 Key Sphere (from Biran)
- **Key Mechanics**:
  - Use Lancet repeatedly to learn abilities — Fire Breath, Aqua Breath, Thrust Kick, Doom, Stone Breath, Self-Destruct
  - Near death, Biran uses White Wind and Yenke uses Mighty Guard — use Lancet after they've used each ability to steal it
  - When side by side, they guard each other — physical attacks ineffective; use Lancet, heal, Overdrives, or buffs
  - When split (one on each side of Kimahri) — unload full strength
  - When one falls, the survivor goes berserk and deals double damage — heal up before landing the killing blow

### Seymour Flux & Mortiorchis
- **HP**: Seymour: 70,000 (overkill: 3500) / Mortiorchis: 4,000
- **AP**: 10,000 (overkill: 15,000)
- **Weakness**: None
- **Steal**: Elixir
- **Key Mechanics**:
  - Trigger Commands (Tidus, Yuna, Kimahri talk to Seymour) raise Strength or Magic Defense — not worth the turn
  - Cast Hastega (Tidus) and Protect on key party members (Yuna)
  - Alternatively: Rikku mixes Mighty G Potion (Al Bhed Potion or Remedy + Star/Lunar/Light Curtain) for Haste + Protect + Shell
  - Lulu's first action: cast Bio — still works, deals significant damage over the fight
  - Seymour is weak to Silence — have Wakka use Silent Attacks
  - **Lance of Atrophy** inflicts Zombie, then Seymour casts Full-Life to kill the character — swap out the zombie'd character immediately; he never casts Full-Life except after the lance; can also cure with Holy Water or Remedy (Esuna does NOT work)
  - If Silence misses, Seymour casts Protect and Reflect on himself, bounces Flare via Reflect — Dispel both immediately
  - **Cross Cleave**: ~2500 HP to entire party
  - **Mortiorchis Total Annihilation**: Charges over two to three turns — to survive: cast Shell on highest-HP characters and have them defend the turn before it fires (turn after "Auto-attack Mode" initiates). Alternative: win fast with hasted Overdrives from aeons + heavy hitters
  - Aeons only get one turn before Seymour banishes them (same as previous Seymour fight)
  - Arrive with full Overdrives for most characters and aeons

### Sanctuary Keeper
- **HP**: Not listed explicitly in guide (appears on p68 without stat header)
- **Steals**: Not listed
- **Weaknesses**: Not listed
- **Location**: End of cavern, before exit to Zanarkand Ruins — final boss of Mt. Gagazet
- **Key Mechanics**:
  - Open with Hastega and Protect on party
  - Tag with Lulu's Bio spell
  - Use Auron's Armor Break, Power Break, and Mental Break
  - **Photon Wings** inflicts sleep, silence, darkness, and confusion — equip resistant armor or heal with Esuna/Remedies
  - When below half HP, may heal itself with Curaga — cast Reflect on it to redirect the Curaga back at it (it may also try Reflect against the party — Dispel as needed)
  - Once debuffs are laid, pound with physical attacks or aeons

## Walkthrough Prose

### Cool Reception
Mt. Gagazet is Ronso homeland; expect a hostile greeting. After the Save Sphere and beginning the ascent, Biran and Yenke demand Kimahri fight them one vs. two. Use the fight to learn every Ronso ability available via Lancet.

### The Ascent
This is a tough area — maps are larger than they appear, random encounter rate is high, and Save Spheres are sparse. Navigation notes:
- Head north past the first ledge, find snowy slope to get atop it, travel south for 20,000 gil chest
- Hug west side while going north to find the Mega-Potion chest (barely visible)
- The path passes graves of fallen summoners and guardians
- Find the narrow north path around a large rock to reach Braska's Sphere
- At T-intersection: go straight for Defending Bracer, then return and head north
- Around the next corner: Wantz (O'aka's brother) — shop with excellent items including Lulu's Booster Cactuar
- Descend from Wantz's location; when path rises again, look for chests beneath the path (HP Sphere, Lv. 4 Key Sphere)
- Continue east then north past a final grim landmark to reach the last Save Sphere before Seymour — **do not skip it**

### After Seymour Flux
After defeating Seymour, search for the hidden Saturn Crest chest near the eastern Save Sphere. In the next area the fayth cluster is found. During Tidus's vision: enter the interior room of the boathouse and stand in the center, then follow the guide outside.

### Underwater Trials — 1st Trial
In the cavern, a hole blocks northward progress. Head west through the cavern into an underwater area. Tidus, Wakka, and Rikku proceed without the others. Time Wakka's throw to hit the spinning shield's opening — no penalty for failure. Prize: Lv. 1 Key Sphere chest appears.

### Underwater Trials — 2nd Trial
Return to the Save Sphere area (the hole is replaced by a stairway). Ascend and head north. The trio splits again to swim. At the end of the second channel: three colored lights. Guide each character into the matching light — Green = Rikku, Blue = Tidus, Orange = Wakka (matching their Sphere Grid colors).

### Cavern Loot (post-trials)
Completing the 2nd trial drops stones forming a long stairway out. Before leaving:
- Pep Talk armguard: alcove northwest of Save Sphere
- Return Sphere + Recovery Ring: aquatic passage to the east

After looting, take the stairway north to the final Save Sphere. There is also a warp panel to return to the Gagazet gates, and the Sanctuary Keeper guards the exit.

## Tips

- **Save Sphere warning**: Save before the Sanctuary Keeper — this is the last Save Sphere before the end of Mt. Gagazet
- **Auron's Overdrive**: Braska's Sphere unlocks a new Overdrive if it is the 3rd Jecht/Braska sphere obtained — consider tracking sphere count
- **Wantz (O'aka's brother)**: Sells items around the Defending Bracer corner — notable: Booster Cactuar for Lulu. This is the only time Wantz appears at this location.
- **Sphere Grid note**: Lancet the Biran & Yenke fight for abilities that may have been missed (especially Mighty Guard)
- **Seymour Flux priority**: Arrive with Overdrives charged for aeons and key characters
- **Saturn Crest**: Well-hidden chest near eastern Save Sphere after Seymour — easy to miss while rushing forward

## Missable Warnings

- **Saturn Crest**: Hidden chest near eastern Save Sphere after Seymour Flux — must be picked up before leaving the area, required for Tidus's Celestial Weapon (Caladbolg)
- **Braska's Sphere Timing**: The Overdrive unlock requires it to be the 3rd sphere — check how many you have before this point
- **Wantz Shop**: Only available at this point in the game; do not skip
