## Initial Airship: Key Events (pp.71–72)

### Arriving on the Airship
After defeating Yunalesca at Zanarkand Dome, descend the broken staircase behind her. The Sun Crest is in a chest at the top of that same staircase. Exit the dome; Cid arrives to pick the party up. After speaking with Yuna on the upper decks, return to Cid. You can now revisit all previously visited areas via the Search command or enter passwords with the Input command.

### Zanarkand Destruction Sphere (p.71 sidebar) — MISSABLE
- You cannot get the Destruction Sphere during the original Zanarkand Cloister visit
- Return to the Zanarkand floor puzzle room after gaining the airship
- A new image appears on the monitor showing seven white squares
- Activate only the three white squares in Room 1 and the four in Room 2
- The Room 1 monitor moves to reveal the Destruction Sphere
- Insert it in the slot to the right of Room 2's monitor
- Reward: **Magistral Rod**
- **This must be done before recruiting Anima** — it is one of the six Destruction Sphere items that lights up a statue at Baaj Temple

### Side Quest Overview (p.72)
The guide cautions: "if you pursue too many side quests, you end up way overpowered for the end game." Available optional activities at this point:
- Hidden aeons: Yojimbo, Anima, Magus Sisters
- Cactuar Village (Sanubia Desert)
- Monster Arena (continue capturing fiends from all areas)
- Omega Ruins (hidden dungeon)
- Celestial Weapons (one per character)
- Jecht's Lost Spheres (10 total, unlock Auron's final Overdrive)
- Butterfly Chase (Macalania Forest third round)
- Dark Aeons (warning: extremely powerful, avoid unless seeking ultimate challenge)

### Punching a Hole Through Sin (p.72)
- Speak to Maester Mika at Highbridge (no weapons needed — he talks willingly)
- When ready for the finale: speak to Cid, select Sin as destination, proceed to the external deck
- Four bosses in sequence on the exterior
- You can return to the airship to heal, save, and pursue side quests between the third and fourth battles

## Village of the Cactuars (p.77)

**Location**: Southern part of Sanubia Sands — West, at a large rock painted with a Cactuar image.

**Process**: Examine the rock to receive a clue pointing to one of 10 hidden Cactuars in the desert. For each Cactuar:
- Sneak up while it looks away; touch it before time runs out
- Success: fight the Cactuar, then receive a named Cactuar Sphere regardless of battle outcome
- Fail (spotted 3 times): receive a "Sphere del Perdedor" instead
- Insert the sphere into the rock to receive the next clue

**Prizes** based on how many named spheres (vs. Sphere del Perdedor) you insert:

| Named Spheres | Prize |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | Potion |
| 3–6 | Elixir |
| 7–8 | Megalixir |
| 9–10 | Friend Sphere |

**True prize**: Clearing the sandstorm over the Cactuar Village, allowing access to the **Mercury Sigil** (Rikku's Celestial Weapon upgrade) and a **Megalixir**.

## "No Encounters" Armor (p.77)

**Ability**: No Encounters — move freely without random battles.

**Two methods to obtain**:
1. Random drop from defeating **Geosgaeno** (Baaj Temple boss), or rare drop from many Omega Ruins enemies
2. Customize onto existing armor: requires **30 Purifying Salt** items
   - Steal Purifying Salt from the Fallen Monks in Zanarkand
   - Or receive 99 Purifying Salts for capturing four of every Dragon enemy for the Monster Arena

## Remiem Temple (p.77)

**Access**: Borrow a chocobo from the Calm Lands trainer; leap across to the temple entrance via a Chocobo Feather in the southern Calm Lands.

**Activities**:
- Chocobo races (prizes)
- Battle Belgemine's aeons for rewards

**Magus Sisters Recruitment**:
- Requires: Flower Sceptre + Blossom Crown
- Flower Sceptre: defeat Belgemine's Bahamut
- Blossom Crown: capture one of each monster from Mt. Gagazet, then collect from the nearby Monster Arena

- Defeat all eight aeons → Belgemine asks Yuna to send her → agree → receive **Moon Sigil** (Yuna's Celestial Weapon upgrade)

## Cavern of the Stolen Fayth (p.78)

**Access**: Exit Calm Lands via the northeast passage → circle past the right side of the Mt. Gagazet bridge → descend into the gorge. The Cavern is to the left of the Save Sphere. To the right (short distance): the **Rusty Sword**, needed for Auron's Celestial Weapon.

**Notable Items**:
- Al Bhed Primer vol. XXV (on the floor somewhere in the cavern)
- Flexible Arm — behind teleport pad in hidden chambers
- MP Sphere — behind teleport pad in hidden chambers
- X-Potion x2 — behind teleport pad in hidden chambers
- Fortune Sphere — (listed in chapter item header)
- Lv. 2 Key Sphere — (listed in chapter item header)
- Megalixir — (listed in chapter item header)
- Mega-Potion x2 — (listed in chapter item header)

**Magic Urn**: Attack one eye for a recovery item prize; wrong eye triggers a massive counterattack. Guide recommends weaker parties simply Flee.

**Boss**: Yojimbo (summoned by the spirit of a dead summoner)
- High physical defense, low magic defense
- Strategy: blast with Firaga and other spells

**Yojimbo Negotiation**:
- Choose the option "To defeat the most powerful of enemies" for the lowest initial price
- First bid: offer half his asking price plus 1 gil (e.g., if he asks 250,000 → offer 125,001)
- After his counter, raise by 1 gil each round
- By the time he proposes ~205,000, he should accept ~198,000
- If you offer triple his asking price at any point, he gives two Teleport Spheres in addition to his services

## Baaj Temple (p.78)

**Access**: Locate via airship search function at coordinates X: 11–16, Y: 57–63.

**Usable party members**: Only Rikku, Tidus, and Wakka (the only swimmers).

**Boss: Geosgaeno**
- Attacks: Stone Punch, KO Punch
- Equip protection against Petrify and Death on all three characters if possible
- Cast Hastega and Protect on the party
- If a character is eaten: use the "Struggle" Trigger Command to prevent them from being used as a weapon
- Rikku can deal damage with Fire Gems and spell-like items

**Post-Geosgaeno**:
- Swim south; when camera angle changes, search crevices of ruins for **Lulu's Onion Knight** (Celestial Weapon, hidden chest)
- Swim north into the temple entrance

**Chamber of the Fayth**:
- Four Mega Phoenix (x4) — southwest corner chests
- Megalixir — southeast corner chest
- Approach the six statues: if any fail to light up, you missed a Destruction Sphere item in one of the six previous Cloisters of Trials
- Return to Zanarkand Dome to complete its Destruction Sphere challenge if not yet done (yields Magistral Rod)
- When all six statues light up → enter Chamber of the Fayth → recruit **Anima**

## Omega Ruins (pp.79–80)

**Access**: Locate via airship search function at coordinates X: 69–75, Y: 33–38.

**Purpose**: Secret dungeon; goal is to defeat Omega Weapon and release the heretic's soul. Also excellent for fast Sphere Level gains and rare high-level Key Spheres.

**Major Hazard — Great Malboro**:
- Can ambush the party; uses Bad Breath to inflict Berserk, Confusion, and multiple status ailments
- Prevention: wear armor protecting against these conditions
- Alternative: lead with a character using a First Strike weapon; they can immediately switch to Yuna (summon aeon) or Tidus (Flee command)
- Always stay in your pre-planned starting lineup

**Navigation**: Map only populates as you explore. Phantom treasure chests found in groups — some contain items, some contain monster encounters that cause remaining chests in the group to disappear.

### First Area and Ultima Weapon (p.79)
- West corridor: follow path to a dormant glyph on the wall
- Proceed north: four phantom chests; **Al Bhed Primer vol. XXVI** is on the floor just north of the chests
- Northwest of that room: a side corridor with another glyph — activate it
- Return to the first dormant glyph — it now works; raises bridge to a suspended chest
- Chest: **Teleport Sphere**
- North end of ruins: teleport pad → chamber above → Ultima Weapon

**Ultima Weapon**:
- Described as "merely a shadow of Omega Weapon" and not much tougher than normal foes
- Equip armor protecting against Confusion, Petrification, Silence
- Inflict Silence and Darkness to hinder his attacks
- Steal to gain **10 Doors to Tomorrow**
- Bribe: 1,400,000 gil → receive **99 Pendulums**

### Second Area and Omega Weapon (p.80)
- Huge cavern; on-screen enemies attack on each connected platform
- Go left from entrance first for a **Friend Sphere**
- Then right/down to reach the final platform
- **Restore full HP/MP before the final platform — no Save Sphere near Omega Weapon**

**Omega Weapon**:
- **HP**: 999,999 (10x the original PS2 version — HD Remaster only)
- Immune to nearly all status ailments — do not bother inflicting them
- Strategy: Shell + Protect to survive his attacks (Kimahri's Mighty Guard is ideal)
- Buff with Haste, Luck, Focus to maximize damage output
- Absorbs ALL elements including Holy — avoid elemental weapons and spells
- Best damage sources: Flare spell, Bahamut, Anima, Yojimbo (non-elemental)
- Steal twice for **30 Gambler's Spirits** each time (60 total)
- Kimahri can use Lancet to learn **Nova** (most powerful Ronso Rage)
- After victory: party is transported to entrance; if you want the chest behind Omega Weapon (**Magic Sphere**), you must walk all the way back and then return to the Save Sphere

## Celestial Weapons (pp.80–81)

### How the System Works (p.80)
- Each character has one legendary Celestial Weapon
- When first found: equipped with 3 empty slots and the **No AP** ability (negative — prevents AP gain)
- To fully empower: find one Crest and one Sigil per character; return to the spherical crystal in Macalania Woods (via rainbow bridge); use the **Celestial Mirror** once for each item
- See Calm Lands walkthrough section for how to get the Cloudy Mirror and upgrade it to the Celestial Mirror
- Fully empowered abilities:
  - **Break Damage Limit** (damage cap raised from 9,999 to 99,999) — essential for Dark Aeons
  - **Double AP** (many weapons) — doubles Sphere Level gains
  - Secret passive: closer to max HP = more damage dealt (not shown as an ability)

### Tidus: Caladbolg (p.80)
- **Obtain**: Race and defeat the chocobo trainer in the Calm Lands (fourth "Chocobo License" test); immediately after winning, walk to upper northwest Calm Lands — the guard blocking a narrow path steps aside; use Celestial Mirror on the symbol there
- **Sun Crest**: After defeating Yunalesca at Zanarkand Dome, descend the staircase behind her — chest at top of same staircase
- **Sun Sigil**: Defeat the chocobo trainer in a race with a total time under 0:00 (i.e., sub-zero final time including balloon bonuses)

### Yuna: Nirvana (p.80)
- **Obtain**: Capture all nine types of fiends in the Calm Lands region; return to Monster Arena owner for the chest
- **Moon Crest**: Chest at a Besaid Isle cove near where Tidus first washed ashore
- **Moon Sigil**: Defeat every aeon including all secret aeons through Belgemine at Remiem Temple, then send Belgemine to the Farplane

### Lulu: Onion Knight (p.81)
- **Obtain**: Take airship to Baaj Temple; dive underwater; defeat Geosgaeno; swim south to where camera angle changes; open hidden chest
- **Venus Crest**: Return to Guadosalam after the Thunder Plains visit; chest in the Farplane
- **Venus Sigil**: Dodge 200 lightning bolts in a row in the Thunder Plains without being hit or leaving the area; collect prize from the chest outside the Travel Agency

### Kimahri: Spirit Lance (p.81)
- **Obtain**: Activate three Qactuar stones on the Thunder Plains (stand at glowing stone, press the action button); find the Qactuar ghost flying above the ground at Thunder Plains — South; follow it to a small ruined lightning tower on the right side; press action button to send a bolt revealing the chest
- **Saturn Crest**: After defeating Seymour on Mt. Gagazet, continue past and look for a hard-to-see chest between the columns
- **Saturn Sigil**: Play the third Butterfly Chase in Macalania Woods (available after gaining the airship); complete both map areas; sigil is the second-area victory prize

### Auron: Masamune (p.81)
- **Obtain**: Acquire Rusty Sword (eastern cliff near Cavern of the Stolen Fayth, Calm Lands); take it to the statue of Lord Mi'ihen on Mushroom Rock Road (previously blocked by Clasko); place sword before statue to reveal a glyph; touch glyph to reveal the weapon's secret location
- **Mars Crest**: Chest in the Oldroad South area of the Mi'ihen Highroad
- **Mars Sigil**: Complete at least 10 Area Conquest and/or Species Conquest missions for the Monster Arena

### Wakka: World Champion (p.81)
- **Obtain**: Win at least five blitzball games; then take the Celestial Mirror and talk to the bartender in the Luca Cafe
- **Jupiter Crest**: Chest in a locker in the Aurochs' locker room in Luca
- **Jupiter Sigil**: Blitzball league prize, but only available after Wakka has won the Attack Reels, Status Reels, and Auroch Reels prizes

### Rikku: Godhand (p.81)
- **Obtain**: Enter the airship password GODHAND; move north to the ravine end; use Celestial Mirror on the symbol embedded in the rock face
- **Mercury Crest**: Chest in one of the sandy whirlpools in Sanubia Sands
- **Mercury Sigil**: Complete the Village of the Cactuars mini-game to clear the sandstorm; access the Mercury Sigil chest in the former sandstorm area of Sanubia Sands

## Monster Arena (pp.83–85)

**Location**: Eastern part of the Calm Lands.

**Setup**: Buy Capture weapons from the arena owner; capture fiends from all over Spira.

### Capture Prize Types

**Area Conquest**: Capture at least one of every fiend in a specific area.

**Species Conquest**: Capture a set number (usually 3 or 5) of every fiend of a specific species. Prizes include:
- Yuna's Celestial Weapon (Nirvana)
- Auron's Mars Sigil
- Large quantities of Supreme Gems, Mega Phoenixes, Dark Matter

**Original Creations**: The arena master creates unique boss-level monsters based on your total Area/Species Conquest count. These are the most reliable source of certain rare items. Capturing 10 of every fiend in every area unlocks **Nemesis**, the most powerful creation.

## Tips

- **Yojimbo bid formula**: Offer half his price + 1 gil on first bid; raise by 1 gil each subsequent round. At ~205,000 asking price, he accepts ~198,000.
- **Anima prerequisite**: All six Destruction Sphere items must be obtained (one from each Cloister of Trials). Don't miss the Zanarkand Dome Destruction Sphere (Magistral Rod) — it requires returning post-airship.
- **Omega Ruins chest order**: Opening wrong chests in a phantom group destroys the rest. The guide implies caution but does not give a definitive safe order.
- **Omega Weapon HP**: 999,999 HP in the HD Remaster (10x the original). This is explicitly noted as a warning for players who played the original.
- **Nova for Kimahri**: Use Lancet on Omega Weapon to learn Nova (most powerful Ronso Rage ability).
- **Celestial Mirror requirement**: You need the Celestial Mirror (upgraded from Cloudy Mirror) to unlock and empower all Celestial Weapons. Details in the Calm Lands walkthrough.
- **Rin reward for Primers**: Visit Rin on the Airship after collecting all 26 Al Bhed Primers for 99 Underdog's Secrets.

## Missable Warnings

- **Zanarkand Destruction Sphere (Magistral Rod)**: Must return to Zanarkand Dome after getting the airship and solve the revised floor puzzle before recruiting Anima at Baaj Temple. This is one of the six statue-lighting Destruction Sphere items.
- **Al Bhed Primer vol. XXII** (Bevelle Temple — Priests' Passage): Only obtainable immediately after the wedding scene; permanently missable after that.
- **Al Bhed Primer vol. XIV**: Conditional — depends on your answer to Rin at the Thunder Plains. Answer "going okay" to receive it then; otherwise it appears in Sanubia Desert — East.
- **Jecht Sphere 2 (Besaid Village)**: Requires defeating Dark Valefor to re-enter Besaid after gaining the airship. If you want this sphere, be prepared for an extremely powerful Dark Aeon encounter.
- **Saturn Crest** (Kimahri's Celestial Weapon crest): "Hard-to-see" chest between columns at Mt. Gagazet after defeating Seymour Flux — easy to run past.
- **Blitzball for Wakka's World Champion**: Must win at least 5 blitzball games. Jupiter Sigil only becomes available as a league prize after Wakka wins Attack Reels, Status Reels, and Auroch Reels — lengthy blitzball commitment.
- **Moon Sigil for Yuna's Nirvana**: Requires recruiting Yojimbo, Anima, and Magus Sisters before fighting Belgemine's versions of all eight aeons. Missable only if you skip the secret aeons entirely.
