## Walkthrough Prose

### Entering Macalania Woods

Go up the sharp incline into the woods. Follow the path to the left and search behind a tree trunk for a hidden **Sleepy Cait Sith**.

Continue along the winding path; watch carefully for treasure chests. **Chimeras** are dangerous foes — use **Auron's Power Break** to manage them. Use **Kimahri's Lancet** on a Chimera to steal the **Aqua Breath** ability.

### Mid-Woods: Butterfly Chase Begins

Follow the winding branches until you encounter a strange bird-like character. Approach the nearby multi-colored (color-shifting) butterfly and touch it to begin the first butterfly-chasing mini-game.

**Butterfly Chase Rules:**
- Run back along the path and touch all **7 blue butterflies** before time runs out.
- Touching a blue butterfly causes a phantom chest to drop.
- After all 7 blue butterflies are captured, an actual chest appears (first area reward: **MP Sphere**).
- **Touching a red butterfly** triggers a fiend battle, wasting a few seconds.
- If you fail, leave the immediate area and return to reset the mini-game.
- Completing Area 1 reveals another multi-colored butterfly in the next region for Area 2 (reward: **Ether**).
- Return after defeating Spherimorph for harder versions with better prizes (see table above).
- Final version (after airship) rewards include **Teleport Sphere** and the **Saturn Sigil**.

### O'aka & Al Bhed Primer Vol. XV

After the second butterfly section, reach a safe area with a **Save Sphere** and **O'aka**.
- If you examine O'aka's inventory without buying anything, he asks if his prices are too steep. **Agree** and he gives you a major discount.
- On the ground across from O'aka: pick up **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XV**.

### End of Woods: Crawler & Negator Boss Fight

**Strategy:**
1. The **Negator** floats in the air and blocks magic and summons — kill it first.
2. Focus **Wakka's** attacks on the Negator. Use Auron, Kimahri, or Tidus to buff Wakka or chip at the Crawler in the meantime.
3. Once the Negator is down, spells and summons become available again.
4. The **Crawler** begins a countdown to its deadly **Mana Beam** attack — a sweeping move that deals extreme damage to the entire party.
5. **Best counter to Mana Beam**: Summon an aeon and use its **Shield** ability to deflect the blast.
6. Note: Aeons like **Ifrit** and **Ixion** can self-heal by casting their own black magic on themselves.

### Spherimorph Boss Fight

**Core mechanic — elemental shifting:**
- Every time you attack Spherimorph, it counterattacks with a spell that reveals its current elemental property.
- You must cast the **opposite element** to deal damage. Examples:
  - Spherimorph casts Fire → attack with Blizzard/Blizzara.
  - Spherimorph casts Water → attack with Thunder/Thunderara.
- Any spell that is not its weakness **heals** the boss instead.
- When you hit its weakness, it shifts to a random new element. Use physical attacks to provoke another spell and identify the new element.
- If Lulu's turn comes up before you've identified the weakness, have her use **Focus** or **Reflex** to waste turns safely.

**Preparation:** Equip all party members with **non-elemental weapons** before the fight to avoid accidentally healing Spherimorph.

**Role of physical attackers:** Their job is information-gathering — provoke the spell, identify the element, then let Lulu exploit the weakness.

**After the battle:**
- Spherimorph drops **Jecht's Sphere** (story item). Examining it triggers Auron learning a new Overdrive: **Shooting Star**.

### Transition: Continue to Lake Macalania

After Spherimorph, the party finds **Clasko** and **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XVI** outside another Travel Agency.
- Suggest that Clasko take up **chocobo breeding** — he will thank you with a **Friend Sphere** later in the game (you can encounter him again on the deck of the S.S. Liki).
- A chest with **4000 Gil** is nearby but **Clasko blocks it** — wait for events to transpire for him to move.
- The Travel Agency has standard features (save, buy, rest).
- **Warning**: Proceeding beyond the Travel Agency starts a major story sequence leading directly to another boss fight. Save your game here.

## Tips

- **Chimeras**: Use Power Break (Auron). Don't neglect to Lancet them with Kimahri for Aqua Breath.
- **O'aka discount**: Examine his inventory, decline to buy, agree prices are steep — he discounts everything.
- **Butterfly chase**: Red butterflies waste time, blue ones count. Leave and return to reset on failure.
- **Saturn Sigil**: Only available on the third (post-airship) run of the butterfly chase. Not obtainable on first visit.
- **Spherimorph weapons**: Equip non-elemental before the fight. Elemental weapons accidentally heal it.
- Save at the Travel Agency before leaving — the road ahead has no turning back point.

## Missable Warnings

- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XV**: Ground item near O'aka mid-woods. Not permanently missable (area is revisitable), but easy to walk past.
- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XVI**: Outside the Travel Agency with Clasko. Accessible on first pass and on return.
- **Kimahri Lancet — Aqua Breath**: Must steal this from a Chimera during traversal. Chimeras appear here; if you skip them all, you can return, but this is the primary opportunity.
- **Clasko / Friend Sphere**: Suggesting chocobo breeding to Clasko is not missable permanently, but must be done on this visit to unlock the eventual Friend Sphere reward.
- **Saturn Sigil**: Requires returning to Macalania Woods after the airship is obtained. Cannot be collected on this initial visit.
