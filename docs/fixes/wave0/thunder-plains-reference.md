## Walkthrough Prose

### Entering the Thunder Plains

Coming from Guadosalam, head down the ramp toward the Thunder Plains exit.

### Navigating the Area

- Head to the left side of the area to find a treasure chest with 2 Phoenix Downs.
- Make for the nearest lightning tower for safety when lightning is incoming.
- Lightning strikes do not actually damage the party — do not let them discourage exploration.
- Treasures are clustered along the southwest wall of the southern map and near the northern tip of the northern map.
- Equip party members with **Waterstrike** weapons — most enemies here are lightning-based.
- Frequent random battles plus many Save Spheres make this a good leveling zone.
- If things go badly, summon **Ixion** — it absorbs lightning damage and is nearly untouchable against most local enemies.

### Lightning Dodging Mini-Game (WAY OF THE LIGHTNING DANCER)

- Players are taught to dodge lightning with well-timed button presses.
- The game tracks consecutive successful dodges (without getting hit, saving, or leaving the area).
- Prizes are collected from a chest in front of the Travel Agency.
- 200 consecutive dodges also earns the **Lightning Dancer trophy**.
- **Tip**: Wait until later in the game when armor with the **No Encounter** ability is available — it eliminates random battle interruptions and makes reaching 200 much easier.

### Qactuar Stones Quest (MYSTERY OF THE QACTUAR STONES)

- Glowing stones engraved with Qactuars are scattered throughout the Thunder Plains (8 stones total, usually in the southern portion).
- Approach a glowing stone and press the action button. Activate **3 different stones**.
- After the third activation, a **Qactuar ghost** appears and leads you to a damaged lightning tower. Press the action button again to activate it — a chest appears containing **Kimahri's ultimate weapon, the Spirit Lance**.
- The chest is **locked** and cannot be opened until the **Celestial Mirror** is obtained (much later in the game).
- Activating the stones populates the area with progressively larger groups of **Qactuar** enemies:
  - Worth large amounts of AP.
  - Their **1,000 Needles** attack always deals exactly 1,000 damage — can kill weaker characters outright.
  - Heavily armored and resistant to magic; use **piercing weapons** or have **Rikku use Lightning Marbles**.

### Travel Agency

- Located in the middle section of the Thunder Plains.
- A book on the counter explains the Qactuar stones lore.
- A book on the right-side table shows your running lightning-dodge count (requires at least 2 dodges in a row to register).
- The counter employee is a **recruitable blitzball player**.
- **Speak to Rikku** inside the Travel Agency to trigger Rin's appearance. Then speak to Rin and choose the top dialogue option to receive **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XIV**.
- Follow Yuna into the corridor; Tidus stumbles into her room (story scene).
- Outside the Travel Agency: a chest offers supplies and lightning-dodge prizes.
- On the ground outside, in a specific spot, pick up the barely visible **Yellow Shield** — this particular one has the superior **Lightningproof** ability (full negation of Thunder damage), unlike standard Yellow Shields that only have Lightning Ward.

## Tips

- Ixion summon is the emergency option: absorbs lightning so it cannot be harmed by most local enemies.
- For the Venus Sigil (200 dodges), plan this for after you have No Encounter armor — saves significant frustration.
- The Spirit Lance chest cannot be opened immediately; return with the Celestial Mirror.
- O'aka is not present in this area; no donation opportunity here.

---

## Missable Warnings

- **Al Bhed Primer Vol. XIV**: Not permanently missable here — it can be obtained at the Travel Agency. However, you must speak to Rikku first, then Rin, and select the correct dialogue option. If you leave without doing this, you cannot come back until later.
- **Venus Sigil / Spirit Lance**: Neither is missable in the sense of being lost forever, but both require returning to this area later with specific equipment (Celestial Mirror for Spirit Lance; No Encounter armor is recommended for 200 dodges). No time pressure.
- **Yellow Shield (Lightningproof)**: Ground item outside the Travel Agency. Not flagged as missable by the guide but is easy to walk past.
