## Boss Stat Blocks

### Spectral Keeper
- **HP**: 52,000 (overkill: 8,000)
- **AP**: 12,000 (overkill: 18,000)
- **Weakness**: None
- **Steal**: Ether, Turbo Ether
- **Key Mechanics**:
  - Fight takes place on six platforms surrounding the boss; party can move between them
  - Counterattacks hit the three platforms centered on the character who attacked — space party members one platform apart to prevent multi-character damage
  - **Tail attack**: Targets random characters, inflicts Berserk — equip Berserk-resistant armor if available
  - **Glyph Mines**: Appear on random platforms; usually only one turn to move characters away before they explode; aeons also affected — expect aeons to last one turn at most
  - Strategy: Start by positioning characters, cast Hastega, cast Protect on physical attackers (especially Auron)
  - Resistant to most ailments (Bio and Wakka ailment attacks are not useful)
  - Vulnerable to Auron's Mental Break — set this up to boost Lulu's magic damage
  - Lulu is key: high Evasion dodges mostly physical attacks and counterattacks; heavy magic damage once Mental Break is applied

### Lady Yunalesca (Three Forms)
- **HP**:
  - 1st Form: 24,000
  - Evolution 1 (2nd Form): 48,000
  - Evolution 2 (3rd Form / true form): 60,000 (overkill: 10,000)
- **AP**: 14,000 (overkill: 21,000)
- **Weakness**: None
- **Steal**: Stamina Tablet, Farplane Wind

**1st Form**
- Easy — hold Overdrives for later
- Physical attack → inflicts Darkness on attacker
- Spell cast → inflicts Silence on caster
- Special technique (e.g. Steal) → inflicts Sleep on user
- Equip armor resistant to the relevant status, or use Reflect shields on attacking characters (Reflect is also useful in the 2nd phase)

**2nd Form**
- New attack: **Hellbiter** — inflicts Zombie status
- Immediately rotate out or use Holy Water on zombie'd character before Yunalesca follows with a healing spell (which kills a zombie character)
- Reflect also blocks the healing follow-up
- Do not cure Zombie too aggressively — you need at least some zombie characters for phase 3

**3rd Form (true face)**
- Cast Haste and Protect on entire party
- **Mega-Death**: Instantly kills any character who is NOT afflicted with Zombie (Deathproof armor bypasses this)
- Keep at least one zombie (ideally two) on the field at all times
- If a character is hit with Regen: use Dispel to remove Regen without losing zombie, OR use Holy Water to remove zombie while keeping Regen (situational)
- Bring in strongest aeons and use all remaining Overdrives

## Zanarkand Cloister of Trials

### Puzzle Overview
- Step on green square near entrance to activate the floor puzzle
- Solution displayed on far wall: step only on panels that form the colored shapes shown — all shapes must be lit simultaneously; stepping on a wrong shape resets the puzzle

### Room Structure
- **First room**: Solve the floor puzzle to open a door to the second chamber
- **Second chamber**: Six pedestals appear — push one of the four corner pedestals toward the wall to trigger one of four similar puzzles
- Solve all four corner puzzles — each completed puzzle places a symbol around the central chasm

**The four corner puzzles:**
- Northwest Pedestal Puzzle
- Southwest Pedestal Puzzle
- Southeast Pedestal Puzzle
- Northeast Pedestal Puzzle

### Completion
- After all four corner puzzles are solved, take the Kilika Sphere and Besaid Sphere from around the second chamber screen
- Place them in the remaining pedestals in the first room
- A Save Sphere appears — this signals the Spectral Keeper boss is in the next room

### Post-Spectral Keeper
- Save and watch event scenes before Yunalesca

## Zanarkand Destruction Sphere (Sidebar — Missable)

**Can only be obtained on the AIRSHIP (post-Yunalesca return)**

When returning to the floor puzzle room:
- A new image on the monitor depicts seven white squares
- Activate only the three white squares in the first room and the four in the second room
- The monitor in the first room moves aside to reveal the Destruction Sphere
- Insert it in the slot to the right of the second room's monitor
- Collect the **Magistral Rod**

**This Destruction Sphere chest is required to recruit Anima** (the game's deadliest aeon). Must be obtained before attempting the Anima sidequest.

## Walkthrough Prose

### Approach to the Dome
After viewing Yuna's dropped sphere and making camp at a familiar hill, the party enters Zanarkand's ruins. The first several maps are completely linear, guarded by the same fiends as Mt. Gagazet. Grab the Fortune Sphere and Spiritual Targe en route.

Inside the dome, enemies become significantly stronger:
- **Defender Z**: Use Armor Break and Mental Break
- **Fallen Monk**: Can be instantly killed by Phoenix Down or Life spell
- **YKT-11**: Eliminate first — their Thrust Kick ejects party members from battle. Note: these cannot be captured for the Monster Arena, so use best weapons freely

### Inside the Dome Navigation
The path is fairly straightforward but treasures are easily missed:
- Upper road layer visible: search east rim for path to upper chest (10,000 gil)
- Ghost of young Seymour appears at an intersection: after ghosts fade, search eastern platform for Friend Sphere
- Crossing debris: look for downward path to Lv. 3 Key Sphere

### After Yunalesca
Descend the broken staircase behind Yunalesca. The area appears identical to before, but climb the same staircase to find the Sun Crest at the top. Exit the dome and head back. Cid arrives with the airship.

After speaking with Yuna on the upper decks, return to Cid. The airship is now fully accessible:
- Return to any previous area for side quests and mini-games
- Find new locations via "Search" command on the world map
- Access hidden areas with "Input" passwords

## Tips

- **Fallen Monks**: Phoenix Down or Life spell = instant kill — efficient clear
- **YKT-11 priority**: Always kill these before other enemies to prevent party member ejection
- **YKT-11 not capturable**: No need to hold back — use best weapons
- **Spectral Keeper positioning**: Keep party members one platform apart at all times to prevent AoE counterattacks from hitting multiple characters
- **Yunalesca 2nd form**: Don't over-cure zombie — you need zombie characters alive for 3rd form Mega-Death
- **Yunalesca 3rd form**: Mega-Death kills all non-zombie characters (unless Deathproof armor)
- **Save Sphere in Cloister**: Appears after placing Kilika/Besaid Spheres — save before entering the Spectral Keeper room

## Missable Warnings

- **Sun Crest**: Chest at top of the staircase behind Yunalesca's arena — required for Tidus's Caladbolg celestial weapon. Collect before leaving the dome after defeating Yunalesca.
- **Zanarkand Destruction Sphere / Magistral Rod**: Cannot be obtained on first visit — must return via airship after acquiring Cid's airship. Required to unlock Anima (deadliest aeon in the game). Do not skip this on the airship return visit.
- **Fortune Sphere and Spiritual Targe**: On the approach through the ruins — easy to run past on linear maps. Check all chests before entering the dome.
