## Side Quest Overview (Airship Era)

The guide recommends pursuing side quests before attacking Sin, noting that Anima in particular can make short work of many end bosses. However, the guide also warns that too many side quests leave the player overpowered for the end game.

Available side content unlocked from the Airship:
- **Hidden Aeons**: Yojimbo, Anima, and the Magus Sisters. Anima is highlighted as especially powerful.
- **Cactuar Chasing**: Return to Sanubia Desert to chase 10 elusive Cactuars
- **Monster Arena**: If fiends of Mt. Gagazet have been captured, the arena owner has a reward waiting. Continue capturing fiends across Spira for more items and high-level combat opportunities.
- **Omega Ruins**: Best training ground in the game; also most dangerous. Accessed via airship. Home to one of the most powerful bosses in the game.
- **Celestial Weapons**: Search for each character's hidden Celestial Weapon, Sigil, and Crest. Each weapon grants double AP, making them worthwhile to obtain early.
- **The Dark Aeons**: Suspicious groups of Yevon loyalists in past areas — run from them. They summon dark aeons far stronger than Sin. Defeating them unlocks a super-boss.
- **Jecht's Lost Spheres**: Collect all spheres retelling Jecht, Braska, and Auron's journey to unlock Auron's final Overdrive.
- **Most Challenging Butterfly Chase**: Now available in Macalania Forest
- **Lightning Dodging and Chocobo Training**: Continue if not yet completed

**Note**: The full side quest detail is covered in the guide's "Side Quests & Secrets" chapter (pages 76–85).

## Boss Stat Blocks

### Left Fin
- **HP**: 65,000 (overkill: 10,000)
- **AP**: 16,000 (overkill: 24,000)
- **Weakness**: None
- **Steal**: Mega-Potion, Supreme Gem

### Right Fin
- **HP**: 65,000 (overkill: 10,000)
- **AP**: 17,000 (overkill: 25,500)
- **Weakness**: None
- **Steal**: X-Potion, Shining Gem

**Left Fin / Right Fin Strategy**
- These are the first two bosses in a three-boss sequence (Fins → Genais → Overdrive Sin)
- The two fights are essentially identical and easier than the third — save Overdrives for Overdrive Sin
- Tidus or Rikku can order Cid to move the ship closer or further from Sin
- **Fighting from afar (safe)**: Sin uses weaker attacks and often passes turns; however, only Wakka, Lulu, and summoned aeons can damage it at range; Wakka's physical attacks without Armor Break are very weak
- **Fighting up close (risky but faster)**: Order Cid to move in, swap in Auron on first turn to use Armor Break; physical attacks become far more effective; Sin's **Negation** ability removes debuffs (Armor Break) and buffs (Haste) — you may only get a few turns of boosted attacks before this fires; sometimes lucky streaks allow many effective turns

---

### Sin & Sinspawn Genais
- **HP**: Sin: 36,000 (overkill: 3,000) / Genais: 20,000 (overkill: 2,000)
- **Weakness**: Sin: None / Genais: Fire
- **Steal**: Genais: Star Curtain, Shining Gem; Sin: Stamina Spring
- **Key Mechanics**:
  - Sinspawn Genais **absorbs any magic cast at Sin** — defeat Genais first before attacking Sin directly
  - Hit Genais with Slow to make the process easier
  - When Genais nears death, it curls into a defensive position and continuously casts Cura
  - Silence Genais to shut off Cura (Genais is immune to Reflect)
  - Continue attacking to finish Genais off
  - Once Genais is gone, advance on Sin's weak point
  - Physical attacks do little without Armor Break — apply it first
  - Use Lulu's Doublecast with the most powerful available spells
  - Bring Rikku to heal and cure Venom status with Al Bhed Potions
  - Yuna: focus on casting Holy or summoning
  - **Overdrives**: Feel free to use them here — there is an opportunity to return to the Airship and recharge before the next boss batch

---

### Overdrive Sin
- **HP**: 140,000 (overkill: 10,000)
- **AP**: 20,000 (overkill: 30,000)
- **Weakness**: None
- **Steal**: Ether, Supreme Gem
- **Key Mechanics**:
  - When ready, speak to Yuna on the external deck, then return to Cid, then head back to the deck
  - Sin draws progressively closer; only occasionally attacks in the early turns
  - At long range: only Wakka, Lulu, and certain summons can hit it — use this time to cast spells and apply buffs (Hastega, Luck on attackers)
  - When Sin draws into melee range: Auron uses Armor Break, then unload all remaining Overdrives and strongest attacks
  - **Giga-Graviton Overdrive**: Sin opens its mouth gradually; when fully open (approximately its 16th turn), it fires Giga-Graviton — an instant game over that cannot be avoided or survived
  - Must deal 140,000 damage before Sin's 16th turn

## Walkthrough Prose

### Entering the Final Sequence
No need to arrive at Highbridge with weapons ready — Maester Mika is desperate and willing to negotiate. The party leaves with the crucial clue needed to defeat Sin.

When ready to begin the final challenge: speak to Cid, select Sin as the destination, then make your way to the external deck of the ship.

The final journey involves four total bosses in sequence:
1. Left Fin
2. Right Fin
3. Sin & Sinspawn Genais
4. Overdrive Sin

After the third battle (Genais), you can return to the Airship to heal, save, and pursue additional side quests before facing Overdrive Sin.

### Combat Positioning (Fins + Genais)
The range mechanic from Sin's deck battles applies throughout:
- Far range: safer, weaker, only ranged attackers (Wakka, Lulu, aeons) can hit
- Close range: faster damage but Sin uses Negation to strip debuffs and buffs

### Overdrive Sin Urgency
The Giga-Graviton timer makes this a DPS race. Preparation is critical:
- Come with Overdrives charged after the Genais break
- Apply Hastega and Luck buffs during Sin's approach phase
- Execute Armor Break the moment Sin enters melee range
- Spend all remaining Overdrives immediately

## Tips

- **Save Overdrives for Overdrive Sin**: The Fin fights are easier — conserve resources
- **Genais absorbs magic aimed at Sin**: Attack Genais first, always
- **Silence Genais near death**: Its looping Cura becomes a major time drain without Silence
- **Return to Airship between Genais and Overdrive Sin**: Full heal, save, and optional side quests before the final gauntlet
- **Giga-Graviton timer**: Track Sin's turns carefully during the Overdrive Sin fight — 16 turns is not many at hasted speed
- **Armor Break is mandatory** on both the Fin fights (for close-range) and Overdrive Sin
- **Yuna on Holy or summons** during Genais fight — do not waste her on status or healing if Rikku is available
