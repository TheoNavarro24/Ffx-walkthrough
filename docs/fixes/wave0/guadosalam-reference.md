## Boss Stat Block

### Extractor (fought at the Moonflow river crossing — entry point to this chapter)
- **HP**: 4000 (Overkill: 600)
- **AP**: 660 (Overkill: 990)
- **Weakness**: Thunder
- **Steal**: N/A
- **Drops**: Not specified in guide text
- **Absorption**: None noted
- **Immunity**: None noted
- **Strategy**: Equip Tidus and Wakka with Lightningstrike weapons at the start of battle. Tidus casts Haste on himself and Wakka first. With Haste active you can attack multiple times for every one Extractor action. If it launches depth charges, use Hi-Potions to recover HP.

## Enemies

No standard random encounters within Guadosalam town. The sphere monitor in the inn teaches you about Larva and Iron Giant fiends (encountered in later areas). Blitzball players present but are not fights.

---

## Key Events

### Rikku Joins the Party
- Tidus is reunited with Rikku on the riverbank outside the North Wharf.
- A tutorial battle teaches how Rikku can steal from treasure chests during battle.
- Another tutorial introduces Rikku's Overdrive: Mix.
- Earning the All Together trophy triggers at this point.

### Customize Tutorial
- After Tromell takes Yuna, Rikku gives a short tutorial on the Customize option.
- With excess Antidotes, Eye Drops, Echo Screens, and other common medicines, you can add abilities to empty weapon/armor slots.
- Prioritize getting Rikku equipped with decent gear at the item shop.

### Seymour's Proposal
- Push open the red double doors to enter the main hall lobby.
- Ascend either staircase and collect the Hi-Potions from the hidden upper balcony chest before proceeding.
- Speak to all party members, then enter the purple double doors to trigger Seymour's scene.
- At the feast, speak to everyone to advance the scene.
- After Seymour speaks to Yuna, return outside the red doors and rejoin the party.

### Brotherhood Upgrade (Farplane)
- On the Farplane, speak to Wakka and Lulu to trigger story events.
- After Wakka's event, Tidus's Brotherhood weapon permanently gains three new abilities:
  - Strength +10%
  - Sensor
  - Waterstrike

### After the Farplane
- Speak to Lulu, follow her, speak to her again.
- Go to the middle level and speak to Rikku.
- After Kimahri emerges from the shop, follow him down and speak to Lulu one last time.
- Tidus gets a dialogue choice: confirm or deny his love for Yuna, or flirt with Lulu or Rikku.
- When party is reunited, head down the ramp toward the exit to the Thunder Plains.

## Shops

### North Wharf — O'aka
- Selling medicines only at this point.
- Guide recommends stocking up regardless (helps when Customize becomes available).

### Item Shop (upper level of Guadosalam)
- Sells a variety of arms and armors.
- If you have been sponsoring O'aka (who is also present in this shop), he will undercut the regular shop prices on most items.
- Prioritize buying decent gear for Rikku who is newly joined.

---

## Tips

- **O'aka investment payoff**: If you have donated 10,001+ Gil total to O'aka across the journey, his prices are now significantly discounted — take full advantage at this shop.
- **Customize supplies**: The Antidotes (x4) on the road, plus medicines collected earlier, are prime Customize fodder. Do not sell medicines before customizing.
- **Blitzball recruits in town**: Several players are here but unless you have been playing blitzball aggressively, most will still be under contract to the Guado Glories.
- **Sphere Grid note**: None specific to this chapter in the guide text. The Extractor fight (Thunder weakness) rewards use of Lightningstrike weapons over magic.

## Missable Warnings

- **MISSABLE — Al Bhed Primer Vol. XII**: On the platform above O'aka at the North Wharf. Must be collected before leaving the wharf area. The game does not return you here.
- **MISSABLE — Al Bhed Primer Vol. XIII**: In the room west of the large red doors in Guadosalam. Must be collected before the story advances past Guadosalam.
- **MISSABLE — Lightning Marble chest (x8)**: Inside the corridor leading to the Farplane — move left immediately upon entering. Easy to walk past if you follow the party straight up the stairs.
- **MISSABLE — Hi-Potion chest (x2)**: On the upper balcony of the main hall after ascending the staircase. Must be collected before entering the purple doors for Seymour's scene.
