## Boss Stat Blocks

### Seymour

| Stat | Seymour | Guado Guardian | Anima |
|---|---|---|---|
| HP | 6,000 (1,400) | 2,000 (2,000) | 18,000 (1,400) |
| AP | 2,000 (3,000) | 290 (435) | 2,500 (3,750) |
| Weakness | None | None | None |
| Steal | Turbo Ether | Hi-Potion | Silence Grenade |

*(Values in parentheses are Overkill AP thresholds/rewards.)*

**Special mechanics:**
- Before Anima is summoned, Tidus, Yuna, and Wakka each have a **Trigger Command**. Use them to raise Tidus's Strength and Yuna's Magic Defense.
- **Guado Guardians** have **Auto-Potion** — steal their potions first, then attack physically. Without potions they cannot self-heal.
- After both Guado Guardians fall, Seymour summons **Anima**.
- Shortly into the Anima phase, check Yuna's Summon menu for an unnamed aeon — this is **Shiva**, newly available.
- **Anima strategy**: Summon Shiva; cast Blizzara on herself to keep HP high while Anima uses Boost and Pain. When Shiva reaches Overdrive, **Diamond Dust** deals thousands of HP to Anima. Repeat to finish Anima with minimal effort.
- After Anima falls, dismiss Shiva. Seymour begins casting multi-spells at each party member.
- **Late-battle**: Cast **Haste** on Yuna to keep up with Life casts. Use **Lulu's Bio** to poison Seymour. Use **Auron's Magic Break** to reduce his spell damage.

### Wendigo

| Stat | Wendigo | Guado Guardian |
|---|---|---|
| HP | 18,000 (1,432) | 1,200 (1,432) |
| AP | 2,000 (3,000) | 290 (580) |
| Weakness | Fire | None |
| Steal | Hi-Potion | Hi-Potion |

**Special mechanics:**
- Guado Guardians provide most of the challenge — prioritize them first.
- **Option 1**: Summon an aeon and use an Overdrive attack to dispose of the Guardians quickly.
- **Option 2**: Steal their potions (removes Auto-Potion), then have Auron and Kimahri cut through them.
- As a final act before dying, Guado Guardians cast **protective spells** and **Berserk** on the Wendigo.
- **Berserk** can be removed with **Threaten** (Auron ability).
- With Guardians gone, manage Wendigo: **Wakka** inflicts Darkness or Sleep; **Auron** uses Power Break; **Kimahri** uses Jinx.

## Walkthrough Prose

### Travel Agency — Clasko Encounter (page 49 partial)

The party finds Clasko and the Al Bhed (Vol. XVI was picked up just before; see macalania-woods chapter) outside the Travel Agency at the lake. Suggest that Clasko take up **chocobo breeding** — he thanks you with a **Friend Sphere** much later in the game. You can encounter him again on the deck of the S.S. Liki. Wait for a few events before a chest with **4000 gil** near here becomes accessible. The Travel Agency has all usual features. **Save your game here** — continuing beyond the Travel Agency starts a shocking sequence of events culminating in another boss fight.

### Entering the Temple (Outside)

Upon arriving, do **not** backtrack for items yet — there is a better opportunity to collect them later. Grab the **Mega-Potion** from the chest to the right and head inside. Collect **400 Gil** from an Al Bhed at the entrance.

Make your way down the ice bridge. Speak to **Tromell** inside the entrance to receive a **Shell Targe**. **Shelinda** is further inside.

Proceed up the stairs toward the Cloister. Witness an outcry from the chamber below. Follow the party inside for a story message. Afterward:
- Check the back of the chamber for **2 Remedies**.
- Speak to shaken witnesses on the right for **2 Hi-Potions**.
- Search the main hall for **2 X-Potions** and **5000 Gil**.
- Talk to people in the **northwest chamber** for an **Elixir**, an **Ether**, and story context about Seymour.
- Chest at the back of the northwest chamber holds **3 Phoenix Downs**.

When ready, enter the **Cloister of Trials** to face Seymour.

### Seymour Boss Fight

Priority order:
1. Use **Trigger Commands** (Tidus, Yuna, Wakka) before Anima appears — buff Strength and Magic Defense.
2. **Steal potions from Guado Guardians**, then kill them with physical attacks.
3. Seymour summons Anima → check Yuna's Summon menu for the new unnamed aeon (**Shiva**).
4. Use Shiva: cast Blizzara on herself repeatedly; use **Diamond Dust** Overdrive to devastate Anima.
5. After Anima: dismiss Shiva. Haste Yuna, Poison Seymour with Bio, Magic Break with Auron.

### Cloister of Trials (Macalania Temple)

Returning through the ice tunnel causes sections to disappear. A ramp appears on the left leading down to the Cloister.

**Step-by-step solution:**

1. Descend to the floor below. Take the **Glyph Sphere** from the central column.
2. Push the pedestal toward the ramp on the far side. Follow it down the ramp.
3. Go back up the ramp and take the **Macalania Sphere** from the raised section.
4. Go back below and place the **Macalania Sphere** into the pedestal.
5. Remove the **Macalania Sphere** from the slot under the entrance, and take it down the ramp.
6. Place it in the slot on the column to the left — a section of the ice tunnel above reappears.
7. Take the **Macalania Sphere** from the slot at the top of the ramp and place it in the center column. The last ice tunnel section reappears plus a floor switch appears. The pedestal is diverted right by a glyph into an ice stalagmite holding a **Destruction Sphere**.
8. Place the **Glyph Sphere** into the nearby pedestal, then push it toward the ice stalagmite. The stalagmite disappears.
9. Remove the **Glyph Sphere** from the pedestal and set it in the slot to the far left. A cube section of wall rises into the chamber above, bearing a new **Macalania Sphere**.
10. Push the pedestal toward the column section on the far right — a section of the ice tunnel reappears above.
11. Ascend the ramp and step on the **floor switch**, bringing the pedestal and Macalania Sphere to the top level. (A section of the tunnel disappears again.)
12. Take the **Macalania Sphere** from the pedestal and push it down the ramp.
13. Descend to the floor below and step on the **floor switch** to bring the pedestal out of the stalagmite.
14. Place the **Macalania Sphere** (Tidus is holding) into the slot under the entrance — the first set of ice stalagmites reappears.
15. Take the **Macalania Sphere** from the center column and place it in the slot to make the ramp reappear.
16. Take the **Destruction Sphere** from the pedestal. Descend the ramp to the lowest level.
17. Place the **Destruction Sphere** in the slot to the left of the doorway. A section of ice is destroyed, revealing a chest containing a **Luck Sphere**. *(Do not skip this step.)*
18. Return to the level above and push the pedestal toward the stalagmites again.
19. Get the Macalania Sphere that caused the ice stalagmites to reappear and place it into the pedestal.
20. Push the pedestal toward the ramp on the far side so it slides back down below.
21. Push the pedestal back into place at the bottom of the column to reconstruct the ice tunnel above.
22. Ascend to the level above and take the Macalania Sphere that powers the ramp.
23. Place it into the last column to complete the ice tunnel.

### Flee Your Guado Pursuers

Upon returning to the temple's main chamber, a host of Guado attempts to block the path. On the long curved ice bridge back toward the entrance, Guado Guardians chase you.
- If one catches you, it summons additional enemies.
- If you cannot kill a Guado in one hit, **steal its potions** to prevent Auto-Potion self-healing.

After exiting the ice cavern, take the **rocky trail on the chasm's right side**. This leads to a partially hidden chest containing a **Lv. 1 Key Sphere**.

Cross the gap to the left side and proceed to the next Save Sphere. The Guado catch up on the frozen lake — **Wendigo boss fight**.

### Wendigo Boss Fight

After the Guardians are handled (steal potions or use an aeon Overdrive), manage the Wendigo with Darkness/Sleep (Wakka), Power Break (Auron), and Jinx (Kimahri). Remove Berserk with Threaten if the Guardians curse it before dying.

### Regroup Beneath the Ice

After Wendigo, use the Save Sphere to recover HP. Speak to all party members. After Yuna leaves, speak to everyone again.

**Hidden items in this area:**
- **Lv. 2 Key Sphere** — partially hidden chest behind Kimahri.
- **Avenger** (Tidus weapon with Counterattack) — chest beside Auron.

## Tips

- **Save at the Macalania Woods Travel Agency** before proceeding to the lake — events escalate rapidly and a boss fight follows.
- **Do not backtrack at the temple entrance** — collect items inside first; the guide notes there's a better window later.
- **Seymour Trigger Commands**: Use all three (Tidus, Yuna, Wakka) before Anima appears for meaningful stat buffs.
- **Guado Guardians Auto-Potion**: Always steal their potions before attacking — critical in both the Seymour fight and the Wendigo fight.
- **Shiva unlock**: She appears as an unnamed aeon in Yuna's menu during the Anima phase. Summon her immediately; her Blizzara self-heal and Diamond Dust Overdrive make the fight straightforward.
- **Cloister Destruction Sphere**: The Luck Sphere (steps 16–17) is the secret chest. Do not exit the Cloister before placing the Destruction Sphere.
- **Clasko / Friend Sphere**: Tell Clasko to take up chocobo breeding. The Friend Sphere reward comes much later (he must first be encountered again on the S.S. Liki).
- **4000 gil chest** near the Travel Agency: Blocked until certain events transpire — do not waste time trying to open it on first visit.
- The regroup area beneath the ice has two hidden chests; check behind Kimahri and beside Auron before proceeding.

## Missable Warnings

- **Luck Sphere (Cloister Destruction Sphere chest)**: Must follow steps 16–17 in the Cloister. If you complete the puzzle without placing the Destruction Sphere, this chest is missed permanently.
- **Lv. 1 Key Sphere**: On the rocky trail on the right side of the chasm during the escape — easy to miss if you cross to the left immediately.
- **Lv. 2 Key Sphere / Avenger**: Hidden in the regroup area beneath the ice. Easy to miss if you rush past without checking behind Kimahri and beside Auron.
- **Friend Sphere from Clasko**: Must choose the "chocobo breeding" dialogue option at the Travel Agency. If you dismiss him differently, the reward may be forfeited.
