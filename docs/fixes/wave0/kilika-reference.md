## Boss Stat Blocks

### Lord Ochu (Optional — Kilika Woods)
- **HP:** 4,649 (Overkill: 800)
- **AP:** 40 (Overkill: 60)
- **Weakness:** Fire
- **Steal:** N/A
- **Absorbs/Immunity/Half:** not listed

**Mechanics:**
- Lulu and Yuna are the keys — keep them healthy with Potions.
- Cure Poison immediately with Antidotes or Yuna's Esuna; Lord Ochu poisons party members.
- Spam Lulu's Fire spells. If Tidus has Haste, cast it on Lulu to increase damage output.
- Use a weapon with the Sensor ability to monitor Lord Ochu's HP.
- **Below 2,000 HP:** he shifts tactics and begins casting Earthquake — summon Valefor at this point (Valefor is completely immune to Earthquake).
- Lord Ochu falls asleep to begin regenerating HP at low health — wake him immediately with normal physical attacks (not spells) as soon as Z's appear above his head.
- **Reward:** Talk to Luzzu after the fight to receive an Elixir.

### Sinspawn Geneaux (Story Boss — Kilika Temple Stairs)
- **Body HP:** 3,000 (Overkill: 900)
- **Body AP:** 48 (Overkill: 72)
- **Tentacles HP:** 450 each (Overkill: 500)
- **Tentacles AP:** 5 (Overkill: 7)
- **Weakness:** Body — Fire (absorbs Water); Tentacles — None
- **Steal:** N/A

**Mechanics:**
- **Phase 1 (curled):** Body is rolled into a ball — only Kimahri's piercing weapons can damage the body directly. Tentacles absorb magic, so do NOT cast Fire at the start (tentacles will absorb it). Target tentacles with spells and physical attacks until both are destroyed.
- **Phase 2 (unwrapped):** All physical attacks now damage the body; Fire still works best.
- If Wakka has Silence Attack, use it to prevent Sinspawn Geneaux from casting Water.
- Use Yuna to heal and cast Esuna on poisoned party members.

## Kilika Temple — Cloister of Trials Step-by-Step

1. Enter the Cloister. Remove the **Kilika Sphere** from the pedestal on the left.
2. Place the Kilika Sphere in the slot next to the door — it catches fire.
3. Remove the sphere (extinguishes the blaze) and carry it into the next chamber.
4. Insert the Kilika Sphere into the slot on the north wall — a glyph appears above the shelf.
5. Remove the Kilika Sphere and insert it into one of the side shelves.
6. Touch the glyph to open the wall.
7. Remove the **Glyph Sphere** and set it on one of the side shelves.
8. Enter the fiery chamber; move right and step on the glowing floor switch — this teleports the pedestal in from the previous room.
9. Take the Kilika Sphere from the right wall to extinguish the flames.
10. Place the Kilika Sphere into the pedestal.
11. Return to the previous chamber. Grab the Glyph Sphere.
12. Place the Glyph Sphere in the wall where you found the Kilika Sphere — this puts out the fire and opens a secret chamber.
13. Push the pedestal (with Kilika Sphere on it) to the floor switch to make a section of the platform drop.
14. Go downstairs. Take the Kilika Sphere from the new slot.
15. Place the Kilika Sphere into the slot next to the exit, and leave it there.
16. Obtain the **Destruction Sphere** from inside the secret chamber on the top platform.
17. Take it downstairs and place it in the new slot beneath the ledge.
18. Open the chest inside the newly revealed area to obtain the **Red Armlet**.
19. Take the Kilika Sphere from the slot beside the exit and continue through the door.

## Walkthrough Prose

### Kilika Port
Follow the party into the ruins of Kilika. The shop and bar are initially closed — continue west until Yuna is found with the survivors performing the Sending. The next morning, before reporting to Wakka, complete these tasks in town:
- West of the inn: rescue the crying girl from rubble west of the docks. She appears in the bar afterward, unlocking an Ether chest reward and the Al Bhed Primer vol. IV on the bar counter.
- North of the inn: chest with 3 Potions.
- The merchant (old woman near the south chest) sells arms and armor — spend only on characters lacking upgrades. Do not buy Wakka's Scout; it is free in the woods.
- Wakka is overseeing construction at the east end of the pier. Visit him when ready to depart.

### Kilika Woods
Head west through Kilika and north into the woods toward Kilika Temple. A tutorial battle triggers immediately on the forest path. Use Kimahri's Lancet on the Ragora to learn the Seed Cannon ability — a good habit to Lancet every new monster type.

Luzzu and Gatta stand beyond the Al Bhed sign. They introduce Lord Ochu, an optional boss that is challenging but beatable. Explore the forest to build AP before attempting him if underprepared. A thin passage to the west leads past Lord Ochu and also holds a chest with Wakka's free Scout weapon.

After the Lord Ochu area: a blue-clad Crusaders sentry gives a Remedy (and a NulBlaze Shield if Ochu is already dead). The second sentry to the northeast gives a Hi-Potion. The east passage holds Mana Spheres x2. Grab the Luck Sphere at the end of the central north-south path, head east to the lakeshore, then north to the temple stairs — where Sinspawn Geneaux attacks.

### Kilika Temple
Wakka meets rival blitzball players; Yuna encounters Dona, a rival summoner. Follow the group into the inner sanctum. When Tidus is left behind, heading back toward the door triggers an encounter with Dona and her guardian. Then solve the Cloister of Trials (step-by-step above). The hidden Red Armlet requires the Destruction Sphere — do not miss it.

Once Yuna has obtained Ifrit's aeon, business in Kilika is concluded. Travel back to town and board the S.S. Winno.

## Tips

- Use Kimahri's Lancet on Ragora in the first woods battle to learn Seed Cannon (and make this a habit for all new enemy types going forward).
- Do not buy Wakka's weapon from the Kilika Port merchant — the Scout is free in the woods.
- Rescue the crying girl before entering the bar, or the Ether and Al Bhed Primer vol. IV will not be available on the counter.
- The Destruction Sphere is required to unlock the Red Armlet in Kilika Temple. Do not exit the Cloister without using it.
- Cast Haste on Lulu (if Tidus has learned it) during the Lord Ochu fight to significantly increase Fire damage output.
- For Lord Ochu: summon Valefor when he drops below 2,000 HP to be immune to Earthquake. Use only normal physical attacks (not spells) to wake him when he sleeps.
- If Wakka runs low on MP during the Sinspawn Echuilles fight (S.S. Liki, prior chapter), use an Ether to replenish so Dark Attack stays available.

## Missable Warnings

- **Red Armlet (Kilika Temple Cloister)** — MISSABLE. Must use the Destruction Sphere during the Cloister of Trials before exiting. If the Cloister is completed without doing so, the Red Armlet is permanently lost.
- **NulBlaze Shield** — conditional on defeating Lord Ochu before speaking to the first Crusaders sentry. Not permanently missable as a category, but only obtainable in this window.
- **Elixir from Luzzu** — conditional on defeating Lord Ochu. Available immediately after the fight by talking to Luzzu; not explicitly noted as permanently missable beyond that interaction.
