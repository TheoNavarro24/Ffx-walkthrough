## Items

### Via Purifico — Maze (Yuna's section)
- **Mega-Potion** — treasure chest just left of where Kimahri is found
- **White Magic Sphere** — treasure chest beside Lulu
- **Elixir** — treasure chest at dead-end south of Lulu's location
- **Black Magic Sphere** — right side of the chamber north of the first Save Sphere (room with active floor glyph)
- **Skill Sphere** — inside the locked room (accessible after activating floor glyph)
- **Lucid Ring** — inside the locked room (one chest is against the locked door, almost hidden)
- **10,000 Gil** — inside the locked room

### Via Purifico — Underwater Channels (Tidus/Wakka/Rikku section)
- **Blue chest** to the right of Tidus's starting position — shop for purchasing items
- **Avenger** — treasure chest in the underwater channels (post-Evrae Altana, if gates not used)
- **Rematch** (Wakka's weapon, described as "excellent") — treasure chest in the underwater channels (post-Evrae Altana, if gates not used)

> **MISSABLE WARNING:** If you use the Trigger Command gate strategy to trap and defeat Evrae Altana in the final underwater chamber, the gates lock behind you and you lose access to the final two treasure chests (Avenger and Rematch) in the channels.

## Boss Stat Blocks

### Issaru and His Aeons
- **Location:** Via Purifico maze, north of the Save Sphere past the red-lit corridor
- **Enemies (three sequential fights):**
  - **Grothia** (Isaaru's Ifrit): HP 8,000 | AP 2,550 | Weakness: Ice
  - **Pterya** (Isaaru's Valefor): HP 12,000 | AP 2,550 | Weakness: None
  - **Spathi** (Isaaru's Bahamut): HP 20,000 | AP 2,550 | Weakness: None
- **Total AP:** 6,000
- **Steal:** N/A

**Strategy:**
- Fight 1 (Grothia): Summon Bahamut, cast Blizzara — sends Grothia into Overdrive, Bahamut likely hits Overdrive too. Mega Flare finishes it.
- Fight 2 (Pterya): No elemental weakness — summon whichever of Shiva or Ixion is closer to Overdrive. Use the aeon's black magic on itself to heal if Pterya is close to Overdrive. Use Shield if Pterya approaches its Overdrive.
- Fight 3 (Spathi): Summon Ixion or Shiva (can self-heal with black magic). Spathi begins a countdown after a special attack — use Shield when it hits zero to reduce Mega Flare damage.

---

### Evrae Altana
- **Location:** Underwater channels, round chamber (save before entering)
- **HP:** 16,384 | **AP:** 5,800 (8,700 overkill)
- **Weakness:** Cure magic (afflicted with zombie status)
- **Steal:** Water Gem

**Strategy:**
- Two Phoenix Downs kill it outright (zombie status).
- Alternatively, use Trigger Commands to strike the locks on the gates behind you — open both locks and flee through. Repeat through the channels, trapping Evrae in the final section where your attacks do more damage and some of Evrae's attacks are blocked.
- **Trade-off:** Trapping Evrae locks the gates behind you, making the last two treasure chests (Avenger and Rematch) permanently inaccessible.
- Enemies in the channels are almost universally weak to lightning — use Lightningstrike weapons.

## Walkthrough Prose

### Yuna's Maze Section
After Yuna receives Bahamut, the party is sentenced to the Via Purifico. Three party members (Kimahri, Auron, Lulu) are scattered through the maze with Yuna.

- Move east out of the starting chamber and follow the corridor.
- Avoid teleport pads initially — Yuna is behind the rest in AP, and wandering lets her catch up. Bahamut's Impulse ability handles all encounters easily.
- Go east then north to find **Kimahri** and collect the nearby Mega-Potion.
- Continue north then west to find **Auron**. Use the Save Sphere here.
- Continue west through the next T-intersection (straight ahead) to reach a locked room — cannot be opened yet.
- Head south, follow the corridor to a teleport pad, turn onto the short eastern path to find **Lulu** and the White Magic Sphere.
- Go south from Lulu to the dead end — collect the Elixir and **activate the glyph** in the rubble.
- Return north to the last T-intersection and go east — corridor loops back to the starting chamber.
- From the first Save Sphere, go north and take the first right turn. Collect the **Black Magic Sphere** on the right side of the chamber. The floor glyph is now active — step on it to move the teleport pad to a new location to the west.
- Return to the Save Sphere near Auron and head west. At the chamber with the gate, stand by the teleport pad — when the glyph arrow points toward the locked room, step on it to teleport inside and collect the Skill Sphere, Lucid Ring, and 10,000 gil.
- Return to Save Sphere and head north through the red-lit corridor to trigger the Issaru fight.

### Tidus's Channel Section
- To the right of Tidus's starting position: blue chest (shop for items).
- Short area — expect only two or three encounters.
- Enemies almost universally weak to lightning — use Lightningstrike weapons.
- Swim forward and around the corner to a Save Sphere — save before the round chamber.
- After Evrae Altana: continue swimming to collect Avenger and Rematch (if gates were not used).

## Tips

- **Yuna's AP gap:** She has missed a large portion of the game by this point. Use the maze wandering (avoiding teleport pads) to give her AP and get her closer to the rest of the party's development. Bahamut's Impulse makes encounters trivial.
- **Issaru preparation:** Bring aeons close to Overdrive before the fight — particularly useful for Spathi (Bahamut).
- **Evrae Altana quick kill:** Two Phoenix Downs = instant kill (zombie weakness). No need to use the gate strategy unless preferred.
- **Item access trade-off:** Using the gate strategy on Evrae Altana forfeits Avenger and Rematch. Decide before engaging.

## Missable Warnings

- **Avenger and Rematch (Wakka's weapon):** Permanently missable if the Trigger Command gate strategy is used to defeat Evrae Altana — the gates lock and those two chests become inaccessible.
