**Special mechanics:**
- Tidus, Yuna, and Auron each have **Trigger Commands** that boost one of their attributes.
- Seymour Banishes any summoned aeon after a single action.
- Seymour's attacks escalate in phases as his HP drops:
  - Below 2/3 HP remaining: begins casting **Break** spells.
  - Below 1/3 HP remaining: begins casting deadly **Flare** spells.
  - Eventually casts **Protect** on himself, reducing your damage output.
- The **Mortibody** casts **Cura** on Seymour each turn as long as it lives.

## Walkthrough Prose

After the Via Purifico events, the party reunites on the Highbridge. They run into Seymour; unwilling to sacrifice Kimahri, Yuna and the others intervene.

- Two **Save Spheres** are located on the Highbridge — one at each end.
- The guide explicitly calls this area an excellent place to level up before the boss.
- **Reflect priority:** If Yuna has not yet learned Reflect, grind AP here to acquire it — it is very useful in the upcoming fight.
- **Overdrive prep:** Get every character into Overdrive mode before engaging Seymour Natus, then save those Overdrives for the fight.

**Boss strategy (Seymour Natus):**
- Open with available Overdrives against Seymour.
- Only summon aeons that can immediately use their Overdrive — Seymour Banishes aeons after one action, so aeons without instant Overdrives are wasted.
- Have Lulu cast **Bio** on Seymour for a steady source of ongoing damage.
- Frontload your strongest attacks before Protect activates.
- **Reflect strategy:** Cast Reflect on your characters — they become immune to nearly all of Seymour's attacks. Cast Reflect on Seymour himself to bounce the Mortibody's Cura spells back onto your party as healing.
- **Mortibody strategy (alternative win condition):** If Seymour is too difficult to kill directly, target Mortibody instead. When its HP reaches zero, it begins draining HP from Seymour. If you can outpace its Cura heals, the Mortibody will drain Seymour to death.

## Tips

- **Two Save Spheres:** Use both ends of the Highbridge freely — good opportunity to grind for AP before the boss.
- **Overdrive prep:** Get all characters into Overdrive mode before triggering the Seymour Natus fight.
- **Reflect:** Highly effective — protects your party from Seymour's attacks and turns the Mortibody's Cura into free healing for your team.
- **Aeon caution:** Only summon an aeon if it can immediately use its Overdrive. Any aeon summoned without a ready Overdrive gets Banished after one attack, wasting the turn.
- **Mortibody kill route:** Destroying Mortibody is a viable alternate win condition — it drains Seymour's HP passively if you can suppress its Cura.
