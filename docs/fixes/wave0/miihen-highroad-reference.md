## Boss: CHOCOBO EATER

- **Location**: Near Rin's Travel Agency, in the chocobo corrals outside
- **HP**: 10,000
- **Overkill threshold**: 800
- **AP**: 90 normal / 135 Overkill
- **Weakness**: Fire
- **Steal**: N/A
- **Additional resistances**: Absorption, Immunity, Half Damage entries noted in stat block (specifics not detailed in walkthrough text)

### Strategy
- **Critical hazard**: The Chocobo Eater periodically stretches its arms and knocks the party toward a cliff. If any character is knocked over the edge, the battle ends immediately — no AP awarded and no Mega-Potions from Rin.
- **Opening party**: Lulu, Tidus, Wakka.
  - Cast Haste on Lulu so she casts Fire spells more rapidly.
  - Use Wakka's Dark Attack to blind the Chocobo Eater.
- **Mid-fight substitution**: Swap in Kimahri and Auron for Wakka and Tidus; keep Lulu casting Fire.
- Use Auron's Power Break to reduce physical damage received.
- **Prone mechanic**: If the Chocobo Eater is knocked onto its back, it loses its armored status — non-piercing weapons deal normal damage.
  - Dealing over 500 HP of damage in the prone state pushes the monster backward, buying distance from the ledge.
  - Aeon Overdrive attacks deal significantly more damage when the enemy is prone.
  - Save Yuna's Grand Summon for when the monster is on its back.
- **Best outcome**: Push the Chocobo Eater off the cliff — receive 2x Lv. 1 Key Spheres.
- **Failure outcome**: Party is knocked off the cliff, ending up at the bottom of the gorge. Must work back up to the Mushroom Rock gates; no Rin reward.

## Team Tactics (guide tip box, p. 38)

- Use Wakka against aerial monsters and to blind Dual Horns.
- Tidus handles agile ground enemies.
- Lulu focuses on eliminating White Elements.
- Auron is effective against hard-shelled enemies.
- Summon Valefor and Ifrit often to build Yuna's rapport with both aeons.
- Mi'ihen Highroad is explicitly called out as a great area to level up for the challenges ahead.

## Tips

- **Blitzball league**: The Save Sphere just outside Luca (start of Mi'ihen) now allows joining a blitzball league any time. Recruitable players can be spoken to with the Circle button.
- **Ropp**: Recruitable blitzball player at Rin's Travel Agency (behind the counter). Level 5, costs 200 gil per game. First recruitable free agent encountered. Press Circle to recruit.
- **Maechen**: Historian on the left side of the road; talks about the Highroad and Lord Mi'ihen. Formal introduction at the ruins of the small tower.
- **Belgemine battle tip**: To win, Yuna or Valefor should be in Overdrive when the battle starts. Valefor faces Belgemine's Ifrit. Use Energy Ray immediately, then watch Ifrit's pattern: regular attack followed by Meteor Strike. Before each Meteor Strike, press right on the d-pad and use Valefor's Shield to reduce damage. Ifrit is immune to the delay from Sonic Wings — attack or cast Blizzard instead.
- **Speak to the kid before the ball**: Talk to the kid playing with the blitzball before interacting with the ball to receive 3x Softs. The order matters.
- **2000 gil chest location**: Easily missed — it is in a small alcove to the right, past the blitzball kid.
- **Chocobo feathers**: Yellow feathers on the ground mark chocobo-only passages. Press Circle at a feather to leap to otherwise inaccessible ledges.
- **O'aka investment**: O'aka is present near the Save Sphere in the Old Highroad gorge, accepting donations. Guide notes he sells only basic supplies here.

## Missable Warnings

- **Al Bhed Primer vol. IX** (MISSABLE): Only reachable by chocobo in the Old Highroad after defeating the Chocobo Eater. On the ground near the Heat Lance in the curvy pass north of the travel agency.
- **Mars Crest** (MISSABLE — CRITICAL): At the very bottom of the Old Highroad gorge. Required for Auron's Celestial Weapon (Masamune). Point of no return — cannot return to Mi'ihen after leaving for Djose.
- **Chocobo-only items** (Heat Lance, Thunder Blade, Scout, Fortune Sphere, Mars Crest): All require riding a chocobo, which requires having defeated the Chocobo Eater. If the Chocobo Eater pushes the party off the cliff, Rin does not give the chocobo ride reward, but the chocobo handler near the Mushroom Rock gates will still let you ride if you did defeat it.
- **Echo Ring vs. Seeker's Ring**: Echo Ring is the significantly better reward; only obtained by winning the Belgemine battle.
- **Gate donations**: The Moon Ring (10,000 gil) and Ice Lance (1,000 gil) are only available from the guard before Seymour arrives and opens the gate. Once inside Mushroom Rock Road, this opportunity is gone.
- **Soft x3**: Must speak to the kid before interacting with the blitzball to trigger the gift dialogue.
