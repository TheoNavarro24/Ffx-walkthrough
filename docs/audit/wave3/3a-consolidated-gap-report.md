### Top 5 Most Impactful Gaps to Fix First

1. **Inside Sin: Complete content rewrite needed** -- 3 EPUB areas collapsed into 1 sublocation, 12+ items missing (largest single item gap), Seymour Omnis Mortiphasm mechanic described incorrectly, kill-door puzzle and Demonolith petrification warning absent, no point-of-no-return warning. (Sources: 2h)

2. **Calm Lands: Complete content rewrite needed** -- 7+ EPUB sections compressed into a single 75-word paragraph, 20+ items/rewards missing, factual error in Defender X strategy (claims Lancet teaches Mighty Guard -- incorrect), Monster Arena, chocobo training, Remiem Temple, and Celestial Mirror activation all absent. (Source: 2g)

3. **Al Bhed Primer numbering off-by-one across 6+ chapters** -- A systematic data entry error where each Primer is mislabeled as the previous volume number. Confirmed wrong in: moonflow (says XI, should be XII), guadosalam (says XII, should be XIII), thunder-plains (says XIII, should be XIV), macalania-woods (says XIV, should be XV), lake-macalania (says XV, should be XVI), bikanel-desert (says XVI, should be XVII). (Sources: 2d, 2e, 2f)

4. **Moonflow: All 3 guide images are from wrong chapters** -- image_0046_00 (Guadosalam), image_0048_00 and image_0049_00 (Macalania Woods). The correct Moonflow image (image_0044_00) is misassigned to Djose Temple. Zero correct images in the chapter. (Sources: 2d, 1d)

5. **Airship optional/endgame: 4 boss cards missing, 2 entire sublocations absent** -- Geosgaeno, Ultima Weapon, Omega Weapon, and Yojimbo fight boss cards absent. Hidden Locations (4 unique coordinate items) and Airship Passwords (3 unique equipment pieces) have no sublocations. No Encounters guide, Celestial Weapons reference, and Jecht Sphere cross-links all missing. (Source: 2i)

### Systemic Issues Affecting Multiple Chapters

1. **Al Bhed Primer off-by-one numbering** -- Affects 6+ chapters (moonflow through bikanel-desert). Each Primer is labeled as the previous volume number.
2. **Wrong-chapter image assignments** -- 9 images are assigned to chapters that do not match their EPUB page of origin. Moonflow has zero correct images; S.S. Winno has zero correct images; Thunder Plains and Lake Macalania each have one wrong Bikanel Desert image.
3. **Missing boss entries** -- At least 8 chapters are missing boss cards that exist in both the EPUB and game data (besaid/Kimahri, baaj-temple/Geosgaeno, bevelle/zero bosses, lake-macalania/Wendigo, airship/Geosgaeno+Ultima+Omega+Yojimbo).
4. **Fabricated content** -- S.S. Winno contains a non-existent Jecht Sphere, fabricated passing drills, and a fabricated extra Hi-Potion. Calm Lands Defender X strategy fabricates a Lancet/Mighty Guard source. Omega Ruins lists fabricated "Master Sphere x2."
5. **Item coverage gaps** -- 15 of 26 chapters have 5+ missing items; inside-sin, calm-lands, and airship each have 20+ missing items.
