# Wave 2 Audit: Airship -- Optional/Endgame

### Airship -- Optional/Endgame (slug: airship)
**EPUB Pages**: 65-67 (main walkthrough airship -- actually Mt. Gagazet content, NOT airship) + 76-85 (Side Quests & Secrets)
**Overall Health**: NEEDS WORK

> **Note on page assignments**: EPUB pages 65-67 contain Mt. Gagazet content (Biran & Yenke, Seymour Flux, fayth cluster caverns), NOT airship content. The actual airship/side quest content lives entirely on pages 76-85. The main walkthrough airship deck/Evrae fight is covered on page 56 (assigned to the `bevelle` chapter audit). This audit therefore focuses on pages 76-85 as the primary EPUB source for the airship chapter.

---

#### Findings

**1. [HIGH] [A. Sublocation Completeness] Missing "Hidden Locations / Secret Coordinates" sublocation entirely.** EPUB page 76 describes six hidden world map locations accessible via airship search coordinates: Baaj Temple, Sanubia Desert (Ascalon), Besaid Falls (Dragoon Lance), Mi'ihen Ruins (Sonar), Battle Site (Phantom Bangle), and Omega Ruins. While Baaj and Omega have their own optional areas, the four small coordinate locations (Sanubia Desert, Besaid Falls, Mi'ihen Ruins, Battle Site) are completely absent from the app. Each contains a powerful unique item.

> EPUB p76: *"Sanubia Desert: There is a lone treasure chest in this isolated area that contains Tidus's Ascalon, which bears the Double AP ability! Besaid Falls: This tiny rock outcropping under the waterfalls in Besaid is where you can find Kimahri's Dragoon Lance. Mi'ihen Ruins: In these crumbling ruins off the coast, you can find the Sonar for Rikku... Battle Site: Proceed north from the Save Sphere to find Lulu's Phantom Bangle, which absorbs three types of elemental attacks!"*

**2. [HIGH] [A. Sublocation Completeness] Missing "Airship Passwords" sublocation entirely.** EPUB page 76 describes three airship passwords (GODHAND, VICTORIOUS, MURASAME) that unlock secret locations with unique equipment. None are documented in the app's airship chapter.

> EPUB p76: *"Password No. 1: GODHAND... Rikku's Celestial Weapon, the Godhand, is in a treasure chest at the northern tip of the area. Password No. 2: VICTORIOUS... Rikku's Victorious, armor that nullifies three types of elemental attacks! Password No. 3: MURASAME... Auron's Murasame, which bears the One MP Cost ability."*

**3. [HIGH] [A. Sublocation Completeness] Missing "Celestial Weapons" sublocation / reference section.** EPUB pages 80-81 provide a comprehensive Celestial Weapons guide covering all 7 characters: locations of each weapon, crest, and sigil. The app's airship chapter has scattered references (Cloudy Mirror at Remiem, Moon Sigil at Remiem, Mercury Sigil at Cactuar Village, Mars Sigil at Monster Arena) but no unified celestial weapon reference. The following are completely undocumented:
- Caladbolg (Tidus) -- location, Sun Crest, Sun Sigil (chocobo race under 0:00)
- Nirvana (Yuna) -- location (capture all Calm Lands fiends)
- Onion Knight (Lulu) -- Venus Crest, Venus Sigil (dodge 200 lightning bolts)
- Spirit Lance (Kimahri) -- location (Qactuar stones), Saturn Crest, Saturn Sigil (butterfly game)
- Masamune (Auron) -- location (Rusty Sword + Mi'ihen statue), Mars Crest
- World Champion (Wakka) -- location (win 5 blitzball games), Jupiter Crest, Jupiter Sigil
- Godhand (Rikku) -- location (password), Mercury Crest

> EPUB p80: *"Each character has a specific legendary weapon that can be acquired by using the Celestial Mirror. When the weapon is first found, it is equipped with three empty slots and the No AP ability. To remove this negative ability and empower the weapon to its full potential, you must find two specific event items for each character: one crest, and one sigil."*

**4. [HIGH] [E. Missing Tips] "No Encounters" gear guide missing.** EPUB page 77 explains how to obtain "No Encounters" armor -- either as a Geosgaeno drop, Omega Ruins rare drop, or by customizing with 30 Purifying Salts (stealable from Fallen Monks in Zanarkand, or 99 from Monster Arena Dragon captures). This is critical quality-of-life information for endgame.

> EPUB p77: *"Tired of having to fight through dozens of enemy counters as you hunt down Cactuars or dodge lightning bolts? There is a solution: using an armament with the 'No Encounters' ability..."*

**5. [MEDIUM] [B. Prose Quality] Remiem Temple prose is adequate but incomplete (3/5).** The app mentions the Cloudy Mirror, Belgemine duels, Moon Sigil, and Primer XXIV. However, it omits:
- The chocobo race prizes (Cloudy Mirror is won from the race but the race itself yields additional prizes for collecting chests during the race)
- The Magus Sisters recruitment (Flower Sceptre from beating Belgemine's Bahamut + Blossom Crown from Monster Arena)
- Belgemine's individual aeon prizes (Lightning Gem x4, X-Potion x30, Chocobo Feather x10, Mega-Potion x60, etc.)

> EPUB p77: *"The aeon of the temple is the Magus Sisters, a powerful trio of warriors who open the temple's magically sealed door for anyone who brings them a Flower Sceptre and Blossom Crown. You receive the sceptre from Belgemine when you defeat her Bahamut, and the Blossom Crown is provided at the nearby Monster Arena when you capture one of each monster from Mt. Gagazet."*

**6. [MEDIUM] [C. Missing Items] Remiem Temple -- Belgemine fight prizes not listed as items.** The EPUB lists 8 prizes from Belgemine's aeon duels (Lightning Gem x4, X-Potion x30, Chocobo Feather x10, Mega-Potion x60, Flower Sceptre, Shadow Gem x8, Stamina Spring x60, Shining Gem x40). The app lists only Cloudy Mirror, Primer XXIV, and Moon Sigil -- missing all 8 individual fight rewards plus the Flower Sceptre prerequisite for Magus Sisters. Also missing: chocobo race prizes (the race has 5 chest tiers with distinct rewards).

**7. [MEDIUM] [C. Missing Items] Remiem Temple -- Magus Sisters aeon not listed.** Remiem Temple is where the Magus Sisters are recruited, but the app's Remiem Temple sublocation has no item for this aeon (only Cloudy Mirror, Primer, and Moon Sigil).

**8. [HIGH] [C. Missing Items] Cavern of the Stolen Fayth -- almost all items missing.** The app lists only "Yojimbo (Aeon)." EPUB page 78 lists: Megalixir, Flexible Arm, Mega-Potion x2, Al Bhed Primer vol. XXV, MP Sphere, Fortune Sphere, Lv. 2 Key Sphere, and two X-Potions (via teleport pad chambers). The Rusty Sword (needed for Auron's Masamune) is found nearby and also not mentioned.

> EPUB p78: *"The Cavern is home to a few rare items, including Al Bhed Primer vol. XXV, and a number of strange fiends... Proceed through the cavern until you encounter the spirit of a dead summoner. The summoner calls Yojimbo... Afterward, you can use the teleport pad there to travel to hidden chambers containing a Flexible Arm, an MP Sphere, and two X-Potions."*

**9. [MEDIUM] [D. Boss Strategy] Geosgaeno strategy missing from app.** The app mentions Geosgaeno in the Baaj Temple prose ("Geosgaeno guards the temple entrance -- weak to Ice, hits hard") but no boss card exists in the airship chapter's `bosses` array. Geosgaeno is a significant optional boss (HP: 32,767 per 1c reference). The EPUB provides detailed strategy including Stone Punch/KO Punch protection, Hastega/Protect, "Struggle" Trigger Command, and Fire Gem usage for Rikku.

> EPUB p78: *"Geosgaeno attacks with Stone Punch and KO Punch, so equip Rikku, Tidus, and Wakka... with armor bearing protection against Petrify and Death, if possible. Cast Hastega and Protect on the party, and make sure to choose the 'Struggle' Trigger Command if any party member is eaten by Geosgaeno."*

**10. [HIGH] [D. Boss Strategy] Ultima Weapon and Omega Weapon boss cards missing from app.** The app's `bosses` array contains only Evrae. EPUB pages 79-80 provide detailed strategies for both Omega Ruins bosses. Per 1c reference: Ultima Weapon HP 70,000, Omega Weapon HP 999,999.

Ultima Weapon strategy per EPUB p79: *"Equip armor that protects against status ailments like Confusion, Petrification, and Silence. Try to inflict Silence and Darkness to hinder his magic and regular attacks. Steal from him to gain 10 Doors to Tomorrow, and if you can spare 1,400,000 gil, bribe him to receive 99 Pendulums."*

Omega Weapon strategy per EPUB p80: *"Omega Weapon is immune to nearly every status ailment, so focus instead on strengthening your own defenses. Use abilities like Shell and Protect to survive his variety of deadly attacks... Omega Weapon absorbs all elements (including Holy), so avoid using elemental weapons and spells."*

**11. [MEDIUM] [D. Boss Strategy] Yojimbo (Cavern of the Stolen Fayth) boss card missing.** The EPUB describes fighting the summoner's Yojimbo before negotiating to recruit him. Per 1c reference: HP 33,000. The app has no boss card for this fight.

> EPUB p78: *"This aeon has high physical defense but low magic defense, so hit him as often as possible with spells such as Firaga."*

**12. [MEDIUM] [C. Missing Items] Baaj Temple -- several items missing.** App lists Onion Knight and Anima. EPUB page 78 also mentions Mega Phoenix x4 and a Megalixir in chests inside the Chamber of the Fayth. The Destruction Sphere prerequisite requirement is mentioned in prose but the specific items from the fayth chamber chests are not tracked.

> EPUB p78: *"Enter the Chamber of the Fayth, where you find four Mega Phoenix items and a Megalixir in chests in the southwest and southeast corners."*

**13. [MEDIUM] [B. Prose Quality] Baaj Temple prose thin and partly inaccurate (2/5).** App says Geosgaeno is "weak to Ice" but per 1c reference, Geosgaeno has equal 1.5x weakness to ALL elements (Fire, Lightning, Water, Ice, Holy) -- not specifically Ice. The prose is only 50 words and omits the swimming path to find Onion Knight, the Destruction Sphere prerequisite detail, and all fayth chamber items.

**14. [MEDIUM] [B. Prose Quality] Omega Ruins prose extremely thin (2/5).** Only 40 words. Mentions coordinates, encounter rate, two bosses by name, and Primer XXVI. Completely omits: dungeon navigation (two areas, glyphs, phantom chests that may contain monsters), the Teleport Sphere from the bridge chest, the Magic Sphere behind Omega Weapon, Lancet opportunity for Nova (Kimahri's strongest Ronso Rage from Omega Weapon), bribe/steal targets, and all strategic advice.

> EPUB p79: *"Areas in the Omega Ruins only appear on the map as you explore them... As you explore, you'll discover groups of chests that are not listed on the map. These chests may contain valuable treasures, or they may contain monster encounters that cause the remaining chests in the group to disappear."*

**15. [MEDIUM] [C. Missing Items] Omega Ruins items incomplete.** App lists Primer XXVI and Master Sphere x2. EPUB describes: Teleport Sphere (from bridge chest), Magic Sphere (behind Omega Weapon), Doors to Tomorrow x10 (stolen from Ultima Weapon), Gambler's Spirit x30 (stolen from Omega Weapon). The "Master Sphere x2" item in the app does not appear in the EPUB or poptracker data and may be fabricated.

**16. [MEDIUM] [B. Prose Quality] Cactuar Village prose adequate but missing detail (3/5).** 51 words. Covers the basic concept (10 Cactuars, Mercury Sigil) but omits: individual Cactuar locations (Tomay at Oasis, Rovivea at Desert East, etc.), the Red Light Green Light minigame mechanic, the Sphere del Perdedor vs. named sphere distinction, prize tiers (1-2 = Potion, 3-6 = Elixir, 7-8 = Megalixir, 9-10 = Friend Sphere), and the Megalixir found after clearing the sandstorm.

> EPUB p77: *"Each time you examine the rock, you receive a clue that points to one of 10 hidden Cactuars in the desert. After finding each one, you are challenged to a game of 'Red Light, Green Light.'"*

**17. [MEDIUM] [C. Missing Items] Cactuar Village -- Megalixir from sandstorm village missing.** EPUB states clearing the sandstorm grants access to both the Mercury Sigil AND a Megalixir, but only the sigil is in the app.

> EPUB p77: *"the real prize here is clearing the sandstorm over the village, which allows you to enter and claim the Mercury Sigil and a Megalixir."*

**18. [MEDIUM] [B. Prose Quality] Monster Arena prose adequate but shallow (3/5).** 59 words. Mentions Capture weapons, area exploration, and Mars Sigil. But omits: Species Conquest (capturing multiple of each species type), all specific Area Conquest/Species Conquest prizes (Yuna's Nirvana, 99 Purifying Salts, etc.), all arena creation boss names, the "Original Creation" tier (Earth Eater through Nemesis), and the mechanic of fighting captured fiends for specific drops. The EPUB pages 83-85 contain exhaustive drop/capture tables for every area -- none of this data is represented.

> EPUB p83: *"Capturing 10 of every fiend in every area unlocks Nemesis, the arena master's most powerful creation."*

> EPUB p81: *"Mars Sigil: Complete at least 10 Area Conquest and/or Species Conquest missions for the Monster Arena."*

**24. [LOW] [E. Missing Tips] Monster Arena "No Encounters" source not mentioned.** Per EPUB p77, Monster Arena dragon captures (4 of each) yield 99 Purifying Salts for customizing "No Encounters" gear. This synergy between Monster Arena capturing and quality-of-life is undocumented.
