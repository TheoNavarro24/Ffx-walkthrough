## Additional Missables NOT in Handoff

From search results:
1. **Wantz on Mt. Gagazet**: Must speak to him during Act 2 story visit. If not spoken to, he disappears and won't set up shop later.
2. **Magic Counter Shield**: Sold by hover vendor on first visit to Calm Lands only. Only way to get Magic Counter on a shield.
3. **Duren (Blitzball player)**: Recruitable at bottom of Calm Lands Gorge before Zanarkand; disappears after.
