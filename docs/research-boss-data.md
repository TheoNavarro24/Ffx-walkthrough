## Boss Strategy Highlights (from search snippets)

### Wendigo
- Accompanied by 2 Guado Guardians (1,200 HP each)
- Kill Guardians first, then use Wakka's Sleep Attack
- While asleep, Haste Lulu and spam Black Magic

### Evrae
- Cast Slow on Evrae, Haste on Tidus and Rikku
- Use Trigger Commands to keep airship distant
- Cid fires cannons if ship stays away 2 turns

### Seymour Natus
- Attack Mortibody instead of Seymour directly
- Mortibody drains HP from Seymour when killed
- Direct attacks on Seymour cause retaliation

### Spherimorph
- Absorbs all elements except one (random rotation)
- Physical attack to provoke elemental counter — reveals weakness
- Cast opposing element for big damage
- Elemental Shift after hitting weakness

### Yunalesca
- 3 phases with separate HP pools
- Phase 2: inflicts Zombie on party
- Phase 3: Mega Death kills all non-Zombie characters
- Key: keep at least one character Zombied going into Phase 3
- Holy Water to cure Zombie, but timing matters

### Braska's Final Aeon
- Phase 2: pulls sword from chest, HP doubles to 120,000
- Yu Pagodas flank him, use Power Wave to heal 1,500 HP and remove debuffs
- Focus fire on BFA, manage Pagodas when needed

### Seymour Omnis
- HP: 80,000
- Four Mortiphasms (discs) behind him
- Casts four elemental spells in succession
- Strategy involves NulSpells and Shell
